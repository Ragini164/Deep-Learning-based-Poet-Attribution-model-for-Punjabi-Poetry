Has khed ke raat guzarya su
Subah uth ke jeev udas kita

Raah jandrre nu nadi nazar aai
Dera ja malahan de paas kita

Agge palang berri vich vicheya si
Utte khoob vichavna raas kita

Berri vich vaja ke vanjhali nu
Ja palang ute khaas aam kita

Waris shah ja heer nu khabar hoi
Teri sej da jutt ne raas kita
