Uthen suteya saij asaadrri ton
Lamma susri wang ki peya hen ve

Raatin kite uneendra kattyo ee
Aedi neend wala lurrh gaya hen ve

Sunji vekh nakhasmrri saij meri
Koi aahlki aan dheh peya hen ve

Koi taap kusrat ke jinn lagon
Ike daain kise bhakh leya hen ve

Waris Shah tu jeyonda ghook sutton
Ike maut aai mar geya hen ve
