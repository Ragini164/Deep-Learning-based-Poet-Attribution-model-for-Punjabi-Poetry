Bhul gaye haan varre haan aan vehrre
Sanu bakhsh le dariye wasta ee

Hathon tereyon des main chad jaasan
Rakh ghar hansariye wasta ee

Dineh raat tu zulm te lak badha
Murren roop sanghariye wasta ee

Naal husn de phiran gumaan laddi
Samjh mast hankariye wasta ee

Waris shah nu maar na bhaag bhariye
Ani mast hankariye wasta ee
