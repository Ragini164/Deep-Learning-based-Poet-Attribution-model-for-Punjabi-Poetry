Hoth surkh yaqoot jiyon laal chamkan
Thodi saib vilayati saar vichon

Nak alaf hussaini da peela si
Zulf naag khazane di baar vichon

Dand chanbe di larri ke hans moti
Daane nikle husn anaar vichon

Lakhi cheen kashmir tasveer jatti
Qad saro bahisht gulzar vichon

Gardan koonj di ungalaan rowaan phaliyan
Hath kularre barg chanar vichon

Baahan velne veliyan gunnh makhan
Chaati sang e mar mar gang dhaar vichon

Chaati thaath di ubhri patt khehnu
Sev balakh de chune anbaar vichon

Dhuni bahisht de hoz da mushk quba
Pedo makhmali khas sarkar vichon

Kafur shanah sareen banke
Saaq husn wastoon paharr vichon

Surkhi hothaan di lorrh dandasrre da
Khoje khatri qatal bazar vichon

Shah pari di bhen panj phool rani
Gujhi rahe na heer hazar vichon

Siyyan naal lattakdi maan matti
Jiyon hirniyan tarutthiyan baar vichon

Apradh te audh dalat misri
Chamak nikle meyann di dhaar vichon

Phire chanakdi chaao de naal jutti
Charrheya ghazab da kattak qandhar vichon

Lank bagh di pari ke indraani
Hoor nikli chand di dhaar vichon

Putli pekhne di naqsh room wale
Ladha pure ne chand ujaarr vichon

Aiven sirakdi aundi lorrh lutti
Jiyon koonj tirangli dar vichon

Mathe aan lagan jehrre bhor aashiq
Nikal jaavann talwar di dhar vichon

Ishaq bolda nadhi de thaaon thaain
Rag nikle zeel di taar vichon

Qazalbash aswar jalad khooni
Nikal dorrya urd bazar vichon

Waris shah jaan nainan da daao lagge
Koi bache na jooye di haar vichon
