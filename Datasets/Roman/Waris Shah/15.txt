Ranjha aakhda bhabiyo verno ni
Tussan bhaiyan naalon vachorrya je

Khushi rooh nu bohat dilgeer kita
Tussan phul gulab da torrya je

Sakke bhaiyan naalon vachorr menu
Kanda vich kaleje de porrya je

Bhai jigar te jaan saan assin atthe
Vakho vakh kar chaa nakhorrya je

Naal vair de riktaan chairr bhabi
Sanu mehna hor chamorrya je

Jadon safaan ho turn giyaan tarf jannat
Waris shah di waag na morrya je
