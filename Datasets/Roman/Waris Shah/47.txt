Luddan kare wiswas jiyon admi nu
Yaaro waswasa aan shetaan kita

Vekh shor fasaad jhabel sunda
Ma'in ranjhe ne jeev hairan kita

Bannh sire te wahl tyar hoya
Turt thalne da sameyan kita

Rannan luddan diyan vekh ke rehm kita
Jo kujh nabi ne naal mehmaan kita

Eho jehe je admi hath aavan
Jaan maal poora qurban kita

Aao karen hain aes di minnat zari
Waris kasthon dil preshan kita
