Darrhi shaikh di amal shaitan wale
Keha raniyo jandeyan rahiyan nu

Agge kadh Quran te bahen mimbar 
Keha addeyo makar diyan phahiyan nu

Ais paleet te pak da karo waqif
Assin janiye shara gawahiyan nu

Jehrre thaon napak de vich warreyon
Shukar rabb diyan be-parwahiyan nu

Waris shah vich hujreyan fail karde 
Mullan jotare launde wahiyan nu
