Nadhi siyalan di viyah ke liyausan main
Karo boliyan ate thatholiyan ni

Bahe ghat peerrha wang mehriyan dehvan
Tussan jehiyan agge goliyan ni

Majho wah vich borriye bhabiyan hovan
Tussan jehiyan barrboliyan ni
 
Bas karo bhabi assin raj rahe
Bhar dittiyan je sanu jholiyan ni
