Pakarr laye jhabel te banh mushkaan
Maar chamkan lahu lohaan kite

Aan palang te kon sawaleya je
Nere vayr de tusaan samaan kite

Kurriye maar na asaan be-doseyan nu
Koi asaan na eh mehmaan kite

Chanchar haariye rab ton darin moiyye
Agge kise na aid tofaan kite

Ais ishq ne nashe ne nadhiyye ni
Waris Shah horin preshan kite
