Heer gai jaan nadi val lain pani
Kedu aann ke mukh vakhaunda he

Assin bhukh ne bohat hairan kite
Aan sawal khuda paunda he

Ranjhe rug bhar ke choori cha ditti
Le ke turt hi pind nu dhaunda he

Ranjha heer nu puchda kon langa
Heere kon faqeer kise thaaon da he

Waris Shah miyan jiven pach ke te
Koi apron loon cha launda he
