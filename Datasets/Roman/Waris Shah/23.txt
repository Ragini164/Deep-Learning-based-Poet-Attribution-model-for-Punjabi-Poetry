Saada husan pasand na leyauna ain
Jaa heer siyal vivah leyaavin

Wah vanjhali prem di ghat jaali
Kaai nadhi siyalan di phah leyaavin

Tain thin val he rannan valavne da
Rani kookalan mehel ton laah leyaavin

Dineh buheyon kadhni mile nahi
Raatin kandh pichvarreyon dhah leyaavin

Waris shah nu naal le jaye ke te
Jehrra dao lagge so ee la leyaavin
