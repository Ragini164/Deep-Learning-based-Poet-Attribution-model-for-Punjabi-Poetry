Chirri chuhkadi naal jaan ture paahndi
Paiyyan dudh de vich madhaniyan ne

Uthe ghusal de waste jaan dorre
Sejaan raat nu jinhaan ne maniyan ne

Ranjhe kooch kita aya nadi utte
Sath ladeya paar mahaniyan ne

Warish shah miyan ludan wadhi kapan
Kuppa shehed da ladeya baaniyan ne
