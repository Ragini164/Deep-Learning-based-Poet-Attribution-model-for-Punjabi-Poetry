Bele rab da naaon le ja varrya
Hoya dhup de naal zaheer miyan

Ohdi naik saa'at ruju aan hoi
Mile raah jande panj peer miyan

Bacha kha choori cho majh boori
Jeev vich na ho dilgeer miyan

Kaai nadhrri sohni karo bakhshish
Poore rab de ho tusin peer miyan

Bakhshi heer dargah thin tudh taain
Yaad karen sanu pave bheerr miyan
