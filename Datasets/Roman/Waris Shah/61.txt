Karke maro hi maar te pakarr chamkaan
Pari aadmi te qeherwan hui

Ranjhe uth ke aakheya wah sajan
Heer has ke te mehrban hui

Kache vanjhali kann de vich waala
Zulf mukhrre te preshan hui

Bhinne waal choone beeni chand ranjha
Nain kajle di ghumsaan hui

Soorat yousaf di vekh taimoos beti
Sane maalke boht hairaan hui

Nain mast kalejrre vich dhaane
Heer ghol ghati qurban hui

Aa baghal vich beth ke kare gallan
Jivan vich qirbaan kamaan hui

Bhala hoya main tenu na maar bethi
Kaai nahi si gal beshaan hui

Roop juttda vekh ke jaag ladhi
Heer waar ghatti sargirdaan hui

Waris Shah na thaon dam maarne di
Char chasham di jadon ghumsaan hui
