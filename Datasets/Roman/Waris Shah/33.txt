Masjid bait ul ateeq aahi
Kahan kabeyon dol utaryea ne

Goya Aqsa de naal di bhen dui
Shayad sandalli noor usaraya ne

Parrhan fazal dars darvesh mufti
Khoob kadh ilhaan purkaraya ne

Taleel mezaan te sarf bahaai
Sirf meer bhi yaad pukarya ne

Qazi qutb te kanz anwaa baran
Masoodiyan jild sawarya ne

Khani naal majmoa Sultaniyan de
Ate hairat ul fiqa nawareya ne

Fatawa barhana manzom shahan
Naal zuhdeyan hifz qarerya ne

Maaraj al naboot khulseyan ton
Roza naal ikhlas pisaraya ne

Zardariyan de naal sharah mulla
Zanjaniyan nahav utaraya ne

Karn hifz quran tafseer doraan
Gher sharah nu dareyan mareyan ne
