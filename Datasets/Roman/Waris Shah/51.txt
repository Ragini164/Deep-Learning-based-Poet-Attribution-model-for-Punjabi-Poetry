Berri nahin eh janj di bani bethak
Joko aunda sad bahaunda ae

Gada wada ameer faqeer bethe
Kon puchda kehrre thaunda ae

Jiven shama te digan patang dharr dharr
Langh nai muhaniya aunda ae

Khawaja khizar da baalka aan latha
Jana khana shereeniyan leyaunda ae

Ludan na paar langhaya us nu
Os velrre nu pachutaunda ae

Yaro jhoot na kare khuda sacha
Rannan meriyan eh khiskaunda ae

Ik sad de naal eh jind lenda 
Panchi degda mirg phahaunda ae

Thag sane thanesron aunde ne
Eh taan zahra thag chanaunda ae

Waris shah miyan wali zahir he
Hune vekh jhabel kataunda ae
