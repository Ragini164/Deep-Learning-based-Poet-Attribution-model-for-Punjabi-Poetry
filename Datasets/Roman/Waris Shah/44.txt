Ranjhe aakheya paar langha abba
Mekun chaarrh le rabb de waste te

Assin rabb ki jaande bhen-parra
Berra thelde lab de waste te

Assan rizq kamauna naal heele
Berre khichde dab de waste te

Hath jorr ke mintaan kare ranjha
Tarla karan main jhab de waste te

Rus aaya haan naal bhaiyan de
Jhatt karan sabab de waste te
