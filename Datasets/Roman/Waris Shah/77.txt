Laai hoke muaamle dass denda
Munsaf ho wadde phahe pherreyan de

Warhi ghat ke kahi de paarr laye
Sathon kadh denda khoj jherreyan de

Dhaarra dhaarrvi ton morr liyaunda he
Thand paunda vich bakherreyan de

Sabha rahi roni nu saambh leyaave 
Akhin vich rakhe wang hairreyan de

Saiyaan javanan da bhala he chaak ranjha
Jithe nit ponde lakh berrheyan de
