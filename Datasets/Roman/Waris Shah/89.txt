Adhi dullh pai adhi khoh lai
Chun mail ke parhe vich leyaaunda-ee

Kaheya mann de nahi sau mool mera
Choori palleyon khol vikhaunda-ee

Nahi chuchke nu koi mat denda
Nadhi maar ke nahi samjhaunda-ee

Chaak naal ikalarri jaye bele
Aj kal koi leek laaunda-ee

Jis velrre meher ne chaak rakheya
Os velrre nu pachotaunda-ee
