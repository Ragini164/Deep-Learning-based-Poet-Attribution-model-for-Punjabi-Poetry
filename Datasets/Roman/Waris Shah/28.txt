Aakh ranjheya bhaa ki bani teri
Des apna chad sadhar nahi

Veera ambarri jaya jaa nahi
Sanu naal firaq de maar nahi

Eh bandiyan te assin veer tere
Koi hor vichar vichar nahi

Bakhsh eh gunha tu bhabhiyan nu
Kon jammya jo gunehgar nahi

Bhaiyan bajh na majlisan sohndiya ni
Ate bhaiyan bajh bahar nahi

Bhai maran te pandiyan bhaj banhan
Bina bhaiyan par he pariwar nahi

Lakh autt he kol wasendeyan di
Bhaiyan geyan jedi koi haar nahi

Bhai dhaaunde bhai usaarde ne
Bhaiyan bhajh banhan beli yaar nahi
