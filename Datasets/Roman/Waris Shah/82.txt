Khawaja khizar te shakar ganj boz ghorri
Multan da zikriya peer noori

Hor syed jalal bukhriya si
Ate laal shehbaz bahisht hoori

Turah khizar romal shakar ganj ditta
Ate mundra laal shehbaz noori

Khanjar syed jalal bukhariye ne
Khoondi zikriye peer ne hik boori

Tenu bheerr pave karin yaad jatta
Nahi janani assan thin palak doori
