Baap hass ke puchda kon hunda
Eh mundrra kit sarkar da he

Hath laeyan pinde te dagh ponda
Eh mahin de nahi darkar da he

Sugharr chatur te aqal da kott nadha
Majhin bht sambhal ke chaarda he

Maal apna jaan ke saambh leyaave
Koi kam na kare vagar da he

Hikke naal piyar de hong de ke
Sotta sing te mool na marda he

Wasse noor Allah da mukhrre te
Manon rab hi rab chitaarda he
