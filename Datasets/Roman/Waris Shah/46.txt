Ranjha mintaan karke thak rahya
Ant ho kandhi parhaan jaa betha

Chad agg begaani ho goshe
Prem dhandari vakh jaga betha

Gaave sad firaq de naal rove
Ate vanjhali sabd vaja betha

Jo koi admi treemtan mard hesan
Patan chad sabh os te jaa betha

Rannan luddan jhabel diyan bharan mutthin
Pair dohaan de hik tikaa betha

Ghussa kha ke laye jhabel jhaiyan
Ate dohaan nu haak bula betha

Pinda bahvarri jutt lejaag rannan
Keha shugal he aan jaga betha

Waris shah aes moheyan mard rannan
Nahi jande kon bula betha
