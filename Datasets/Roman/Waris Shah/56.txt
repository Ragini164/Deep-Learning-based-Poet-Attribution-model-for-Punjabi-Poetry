Kahi heer di kare tareef shaiyr
Mathe chamakda husn mehtaab da ji

Khooni chundiyan raat jyon chann davale
Surkh rang jyon rang shahab da ji

Nain nargissi mirg mamolrr de
Gallhaan tehkiyan phul gulaab da ji

Bhavaan wang kamaan lahore dissan
Koi husn na ant hisaab da ji

Surmaan nainan di dhar vich phab rahya
Charrhya hind te kattak punjab da ji

Khuli tarinjanan vich lattakdi he
Hathi mast jyon phire nawab da ji

Chehre sohne te khaal khat bunde
Khush-khat jyon harf kitaab da ji

Jehrre vekhne de reejhvan aahe
Wadda waada tinhaan de baab da ji

Chalo laila tu qadar di karo ziyarat
Waris shah eh kamm sawab da ji
