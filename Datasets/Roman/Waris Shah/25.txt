Keha bhirr machaiyo ee lucheya ve
Matha dahiyo ee sonkana wang keha

Ja sajra kam gawa nahi
Ho jasya jobna fer keha

Ranjhe kha ghussa sir dhol mari
Kehi chambrri ann nu wang leeha

Tussin des rakho assin chad challe
Laah jhagrra bhabiye gal eha

Hath pakarr ke juttiyan maar bukkal
Ranjha ho turya Waris Shah jeha
