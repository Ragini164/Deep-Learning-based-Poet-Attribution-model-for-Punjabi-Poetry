Heer aakheya ranjhna bura kito
Tain taan puchna si duhraaike te

Main taan jaanda nahi saan eho soonha
Kher mangeya su methon aaiyke te

Kher lendo hi pichaan nu turt bhanna
Uth wageya kand walaiyke te

Nerre janda hai jaa hil nadhiye ni
Jaa puch le gal samjhaiyke te

Waris Shah miyan is thin gal puchin
Do tin addiyan hik te laaiyke
