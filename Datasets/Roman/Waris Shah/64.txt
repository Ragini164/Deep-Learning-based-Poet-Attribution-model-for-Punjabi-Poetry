Maan mattiye roop gumaan bhariye
Athkeliye rang rangeeliye ni

Aashiq bhor faqeer te naag kaale
Bajh mantran mool na keeliye ni

Eh jobna thag bazar da ee
Toone haariye chel chabeeliye ni

Tere palang da rang na wap ghatteya
Na kar shohdeyan naal bakheeliye ni

Waris Shah bin kardon zibah kariye
Bol naal zuban raseeliye ni
