Aakhan devare naal nihaal hoiyan
Sanu sab shareekniyan hasdiyan ne

Eh ranhe de naal hen gheyo shakar
Par jeev da bhed na dasdiyan ne

Ranna digdiyan vekh ke chail munda
Jiven shehed vich makhiyan phasdiyan ne

Ik tu kalank hain assan lagga
Hor sab sukhaliyan wasdiyan ne

Gharon niklen te peya maren bhukka
Waris bhul javan kharmastiyan ne
