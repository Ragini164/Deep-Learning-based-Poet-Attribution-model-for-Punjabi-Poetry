Sidha ho ke rottiyan khaa jatta
Bhuwan kasnu aidiyan chaiyan ni

Teri panghattan de utte peyo pai
Dhumman tarinjana de vich paiyan ni

Ghar baar visaar ke khawar hoiyan
Jhokan prem diyan jinhaan nu laiyan ni

Zulfaan kundiyan kaliyan haonk mangu
Jhokaan hik te aan bithaiyan ni

Waris shah eh jinhaan de chann devar
Ghol ghattiyan oh bharjaiyan ni
