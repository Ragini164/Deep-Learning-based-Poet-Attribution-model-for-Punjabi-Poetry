Jit dihaarre dhan wari
Saahe laye likhai
Malak je kanni sunenda
Maunh dikhale aai
Jind nimaani kadhiye
Haddan kun karrkaai
Saahe likhe na challani
Jindo ku samjhai
Aapan hathi jol ke
Kain gal lagge dhaai
Walon nikki pursalat
Kanni na suni aai
Fareeda kirri paundi ee
Kharra na aap muhaai
