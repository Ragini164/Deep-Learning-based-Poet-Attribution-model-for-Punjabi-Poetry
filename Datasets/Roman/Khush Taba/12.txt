Seen surt sambhal hun khol akhin, agge neendran laiyaan batheriyan ni
Khana sutt ke chorr chapatt suton, eh achiyan nahi daleriyan ni
Tera maal mata sab luttne nu, bahin dakuwan vekh holeriyan ni
Zaiye geya je maal te theek janen, pher shamtan auniyan teriyan ni
Hun vi jaag ke maal sambhal apna, kookan suni naseehtan teriyan ni
Jo jo karan pukar tun ghook sonven, akhin meetiyan hor wadheiyan ni
Jaag waqt koolrre jaen kidhar, jadon chaliyan qeher hanairiyan ni
Khush taba dilgeer akheer hoiyaan, akhin sahab ton jihnan ne pheriyan ni
