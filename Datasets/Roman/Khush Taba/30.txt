Ye yaad kar kar rovaan dine raatin hoye aib methon be-andaz Allah
Apne fazal de naal cha karen bakhshen, mangaan dua kar dast daraz Allah
Nikle naal imaan wajood vichon, kar jaye jad rooh parvaz Allah
Hon dam jaan aake khatam mere, hovaan vich sujood namaz Allah
Rakhin shaad abaad sab dajna nu, dard khawah jitne mehram raz Allah
Deven naik hidayat kapateyan nu, aa jaan shararton baz Allah
Sadqe nabi de karin qabool sari main ghareeb di ijz niyaz Allah
Khush taba tasneed sun ke meri, tere ishq vich hon gudaz Allah
