Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib de darbaron,
Pairo nangi siron jhandoli suneha aaya paron,
Tadbarat kujh ban'da nahi ki laisaan sansaron,
Main pakrann chhajni hirsa udaavaan chhata malgazaron,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib de darbaron.

Hindu Turk na hunde aise do jarme taray jarme,
Haram halal pachhata nahi asi dohaan te nahi bharme,
Guru peer di parakh asanu sabhna ton sir vaaro,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib de darbaron.

Ghundi mundi da buhal bhaaya bakra liya deedaron,
Ghund mukh piya ton laaya sharam rahi darbaron,
Bullah Shah de ho ke rahiye chhutt gaye kaar bagaron,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib de darbaron.
