Bahurii ve tabiba maindi jind gayia,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Ishq dera mere andar kita,
Bhar ke zahar piala main peeta,
Jhabde aavi ve tabiba nahi te main mari gayia,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Chhup gaya sooraj bahar rahi gayi aa laali,
Hova main sadke murh je den vikhali,
Main bhull gayia tere naal na gayia,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Tere ishq di saar ve main na jana,
Ih sir aaya ae mera heth vadana,
Sat pai ishke di taan kookan daiyan,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Tere ishqon kolon saanu hatk na maaye,
Laahore jaande bede mor kon hataye,
Meri akal bhulli naal mohaniyan de gayia,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Is ishq di jhangi vich mor bulenda,
Saano kaaba te qibla pyara yaar dasenda,
Saano ghayal karke phir khabar na layia,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.

Bulleh Shah Inayat de bhe bohe,
Jis pehnaaye saanu saave suhe,
Jaan main mari udari mil pia vehiya,
Tere ishq nachaya kar thaia thaia.
