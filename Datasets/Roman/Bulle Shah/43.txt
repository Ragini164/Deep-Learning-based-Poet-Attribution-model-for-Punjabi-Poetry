Hun main likhya sohna yaar.
Jis de husn da garam bazaar.

Jad ahad ik ikalla si, na zahar koi tajjalla si,
Na Rab Rasool na Allah si, na jabar te na kahar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Bechoon wa bechgoona si, beshbeeh benamoona si,
Na koi rang na namoona si, hun gunaan-gun hazaar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Pyaara pehen poshakaan aaya, Adam apna naam dharaaya,
Ahad te ban Ahmad aaya, Nabiyan da sardaar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Kun keha faajikun kehaaya, bechooni se chooni banaaya,
Ahad de vich meem ralaaya, taan kita add pasaara.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Tajoon masjid tajoon butkhana, barte rehan na roza jaana,
Bhool gaya wuzu namaaz dugaana, tain par jaan karaan balihaar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Peer paighamber isde barde, is malaaik sajde karde,
Sar kadmaan de utte dharde, sab ton vaddi oh sarkar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.

Jo koi us nu likhya chahe, baajh wasile likhya na jaaye,
Shah Anaait bhet bataaye, taan khulle sabh israar.
Hun main likhya sohna yaar.
