Teriyan vaatan to sir vaaria.

Chadh baagi koyal kookdi,
Nit soz-e-alam de phookdi,
Mainu tattari ko shaam visaaria.
Kadi aa mil yaar pyaaria,
Teriyan vaatan to sir vaaria.

Bullha shahu kadi ghar aavsi,
Meri baldi bha bujhavsi,
Udhi vaatan to sir vaaria.

Kadi aa mil yaar pyaaria,
Teriyan vaatan to sir vaaria.
