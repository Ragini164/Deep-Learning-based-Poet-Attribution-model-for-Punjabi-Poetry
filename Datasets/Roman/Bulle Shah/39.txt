Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga
Gal alfi sir-pa-barhina, bhalkay roop vataavenga,
Is lalach nafsani kolon, orak moon manaavenga,
Ghat zikat mangange piade, kaho ki amal vikhaavenga,
Aan bani sir par bhari, agon ki batlavega,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Hak paraaya jaato nahi, kha kar bhaar uthavenga,
Phir na a kar badla desen laakhi khet lutaavenga,
Daa la ke vich jag de juue, jitte dam haravenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Jaisi karni vaisi bharni, prem nagar da vartaara ae,
Ethay dozakh kat tu dilbar, age khul bahara ae,
Kesar bij jo kesar janme, lasan bij ki khavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Karo kamaai mere bhai, iho vakat kamaavan da,
Pau-sataaraan painde ne hun, daa na baazi haaran da,
Ujri khed chhapan giyan, jhaadu kaan uthaavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Khaven maas chhavaven beerhe, ang pushak lagaaya ee,
Teedhi pagri akkad chalen, jutti pair adhaaya ee,
Palda hai tu jam da bakra, aapna aap kuhavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Pal da vaasa vassan ethay, rahin nu age deraa ae,
Lai lai tuhfe ghar nu ghalleen, iho vela tera ae,
Othe hath na lagda kujh vi, etho hi lai javaenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Parh sabak muhabbat os da tu, bemoojib kyun dubna ae,
Parh parh kise magaz khavaaven, kyun khuban vich khubna ae,
Harf ishq da ikko nukta, kahe ko uth ladaavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Bhukh marenda naam Allah da, iho baat changeri ae,
Dove thok pathar thein bhare, aukhi jihi iho pheri ae,
Aan bani jad sir par bhari, aggon ki batlavenge,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Amaan baba beti beta, puchh vekhan kyun rondhe ne,
Rannaan kanjkaan bhaina bhai, vaaras aan khulonde ne,
Iho jo lutde tu nahi lutda, mar ke aap lutavaenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Ik ikalleaan jaana ae tain, naal na koi jaavega,
Khavesh-kabeela ronda pitda, raaho hi murr aavega,
Shehron baahar jungle vich vaase, othe dera paavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Karaan naseehat vaddi je koi, sun kar dil te laavenga,
Mohe taan roz-hashr nu utthan, aashiq na mar jaavenga,
Je tu maarein marn toh agge, marne da mull paavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Kahindi hai par kardi nahi, iho khalqat rovenga,
Hun suttiyaan tainu kaun jagaye, jagdiyan pachhtaavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Je tu saade akhe lagge, tainu takht bahaavange,
Jis nu saara aalam dhounde, tainu aan milaavange,
Zuhdi ho ke zuhd kamaavein, lai pia gal laavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Aiven umar gawaiya aogat, akbat cha rurrhaaya ae,
Laalach kar kar duniyan utte, mukh safedi aaya ae,
Ajje vi sun je taib hove, taan ashna sadaavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.

Bullah shaoh de chalna ae taan chal, kiha chir laaya ae,
Jiko dhakke ki karne, jaan vatano dafatar aaya ae,
Wachandiaan khat akal gayiyo ae, ro ro haal vanjaavenga,
Hijab kare darveshi kolon, kad tak hukam chalavenga.
