Hun mainu kaun pachhaane, hun main ho gayi ni kujh hor.
Haadi mainu sabak padhaaya, othe gair na aaya jaaya,
Matalak jaat jamal dikhaaya, vahdat paaya shor.
Hun mainu kaun pachhaane, hun main ho gayi ni kujh hor.

Awwal ho ke laamkaani, zahar batan disda jaani,
Riha na mera naam nishaani, mit gaya jhagda shor.
Hun mainu kaun pachhaane, hun main ho gayi ni kujh hor.

Pyaara aap jamal vikhale, mast kalandar hon matwale,
Hansan de hun vekh lai chaale, Bulle shor gayi tor.
Hun mainu kaun pachhaane, hun main ho gayi ni kujh hor.
