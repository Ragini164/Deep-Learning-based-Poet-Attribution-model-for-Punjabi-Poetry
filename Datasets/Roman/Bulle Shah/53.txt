Saabir ne jad nehu lagaya, Dekh peea ne ki dikhlaya,
Rag rag andar karam chalaya, Zorawar di gal muhaal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.

Zakria ne jad paya khaara, Jis dam vajya ishq nagara,
Dharya sir te tikha aara, Ki kita add javal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.

Jado yehiye ne payi jhaati, Ramz ishq di layi kaati,
Jalwa ditta apna jaati, Tan khanjar kita laal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.

Ap ishaara akh da kita, Taan madhva Mansoor ne peeta,
Sooli chadh ke darshan leeta, Hoaya ishq kamal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.

Suleman nu ishq jo aaya, Mundra hatho cha gavaya,
Takht na pareya da phir aaya, Bhath jhoke pia behaal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.

Bulleh Shah hun chup changeri, Na kar etho add dalairi,
Gal na bandi teeri meri, Chhad de saare veham khyaal,
Is nehu di ultee chaal.
