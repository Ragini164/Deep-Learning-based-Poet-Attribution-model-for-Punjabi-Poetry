Ih Dukh Ja Kaho Kis Aage,
Rom Rom Gha Prem Ke Laage.

Ik Marna Dooja Jagg Di Haasi.
Kart Phirt Nit Mohi Re Mohi.

Kaun Kare Mohe Se Diljoi.
Shaam Piya Main Ditti Haan Dharoi.

Dukh Jagg Ke Mohe Poochhan Aaye.
Jin Ko Piya Pardes Sidhaaye.

Na Piya Jaaye Na Piya Aaye.
Ih Dukh Ja Kaho Kis Jaaye.

Bullha Shah Ghar Aa Pyaaria.
Ik Ghadi Ko Karan Guzaaria.
Tan Man Dhan Jiya Tain Par Vaaria.

Ih Dukh Ja Kaho Kis Aage,
Rom Rom Gha Prem Ke Laage.
