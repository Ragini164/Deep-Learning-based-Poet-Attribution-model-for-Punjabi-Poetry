Khaki khak soon (siun) ral jaana
Kuch nahin zor dhyaana.

Gaye so gaye phir nahi aaye, mere jaani meet pyaare,
Mere baajhon rahinde nahin, hun kyun asaan visaare,
Khaki khak soon (siun) ral jaana.

Chit pyaar na jaaye saatho, ubhe saah na rahinde,
Asi moyaan de parle paar, jiunde de vich bahinde,
Khaki khak soon (siun) ral jaana.

Othe magar piyade lage, taan asi ethay aaye,
Ethay saanu rahin na milda, agge kit wal dhaaye,
Khaki khak soon (siun) ral jaana.

Bulle ethay rahin na milda, ronde pitde challe,
Iko naam ose da kharchi, paisa hor na palley,
Khaki khak soon (siun) ral jaana
Na kar zor dhyaana.
