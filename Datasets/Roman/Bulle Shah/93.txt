Mainu Sukh Da Sunehera Tu Jhab Lyaavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.

Main Kubri Main Dubri Hoyian,
Mere Sabh Dukhde Batlaavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.

Khulhian Litaan Gal Dast Paraanda,
Eh Gall Akhi Na Sharmaavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.

Ik Lakh Dendi Main Do Lakh Desaan,
Mainu Pyaara Aan Milaavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.

Yaara Likhi Kitaabat Bheji,
Kithe Goshe Baha Samjhavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.

Bullah Shah Diyan Mudan Muhaaraan,
Lik Patiaan Tu Jhab Paavi Ve,
Mainu Sukh Da Sunehera Tu Jhab Lyaavi Ve,
Paandhia Ho.
