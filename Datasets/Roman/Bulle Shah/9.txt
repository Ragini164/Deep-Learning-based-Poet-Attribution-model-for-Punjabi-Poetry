Aisa man mein aayo re, dukh sukh sabh vannayo re.
Haar shingar ko aag lagaun, tan par dhaand machaayo re.

Sun ke gyaan ki aisi baatan, naam-nishaan tabhi alghaataan.
Koil vaangun main kookan raatan, tain aje bhi taras na aayo re.

Gal mirgaani siis khappariyan, darshan ki bheekh mangna charhia.
Jogan naam boohlat dhariya, ang bibhoot ramaayo re.

Ishq mullaan ne baang sunaai, ih gall vaajab aayi.
Kar kar sidak sijdhe wal dhaai, moonh mahiraab tikayo re.

Prem nagar waale ulte chaale, main moii bhar khushiyan naale.
Aan fasi aape vich jaale, hass hass aap kuhaayo re.

Bulle shauh sang preet lagai, jeean jaame di ditti saai.
Murshad Shah Inayat sai, jis dil mera bharamayo re.
