Ik alaf parho chhutkara ae
Ik alafon do tin chaar hoye, phir lakh karod hazaar hoye,
Phir uthon baadon shumaar hoye, ik alaf da nuqta nyaara ae,
Ik alaf parho chhutkara ae.

Kyun parhna ain gadd kitaabaan di, sir chaana ain pand azaabaan di,
Hun hoyun shakal jalaadaan di, agge painda mushkil bhaara ae,
Ik alaf parho chhutkara ae.

Ban haafiz hifz Quran karein, parh parh ke saaf zabana karein,
Phir niyamat vich dhyaan karein, man phirda jioun halkara ae,
Ik alaf parho chhutkara ae.

Bulle bhi bohr da boiyea ae, uh birch vadda ja hoya ae,
Jad birch uh faani hoya ae, phir rah gaya bhi akaara ae,
Ik alaf parho chhutkara ae.
