Main paya ae, main paya ae,
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.

Kahoon tark kitaaban parhde ho, kahoon bhagat Hindu Jap karte ho,
Kahoon Gorkandi vich parhde ho, har ghar ghar laad ladaya ae.
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.

Kitte veeri ho, kitte beli ho, kitte aap Guru kitte cheli ho,
Kitte Majnu ho, kitte Laili ho, har ghat ghat beech samaaya ae.
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.

Kahoon ghaafil, kahoon namaazi ho, kahoon mimbar te bai kaazi ho,
Kahoon Tegh Bahadur Ghazi ho, kahoon apna panth banaaya ae.
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.

Kahoon masjid da wartaara ae, kahoon baniya Thakurdwara ae,
Kahoon bairaagi jatt dhaara ae, kahoon Shaikh ban ban aaya ae.
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.

Bulla Shah da main muhtaj hoaya, Maharaj mile mera kaaj hoaya,
Mujhe piya ka darshan mi'raaj hoaya, laga ishq tan yeh gun gaaya ae.
Main paya ae, main paya ae,
Tain aap saroop vataaya ae.
