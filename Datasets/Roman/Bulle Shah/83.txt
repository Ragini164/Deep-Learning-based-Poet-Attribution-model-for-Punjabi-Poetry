Main kusumbra chun chun haari,
Ae kusumbe de kande bhallery ad ad chunnri paari.
Ae kusumbe da hakam karrha zalma ae patvaari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.

Ae kusumbe de chaar mukaddam muaamla mangde bhaari,
Hornan chuggiya fuhiya-fuhiya, main bhar lai patari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.

Chugg chugg ke main dheri kiita lathhe aan bapari,
Aukhi ghati mushkal painda, sir 'te gathri bhaari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.

Amlaan vaalian sab langh gayian, rah gayi augunhaari.
Saari umra khed gawai, orak baazi haari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.

Allast keha jad akhian laayian, hun kyun yaar visaari.
Ikko ghar vich vasdiyan, rasdiyan, hun kyun raahi nyari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.

Main kameeni kuchji kohji, begunn kaun vichaari.
Bulle Shah shauh de layik nahi, Shah Inayat taari.
Main kusumbra chun chun haari.
