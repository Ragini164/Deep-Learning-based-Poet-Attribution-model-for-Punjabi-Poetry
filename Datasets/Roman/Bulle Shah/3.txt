Aao saiyo ral diyo ni vadhai.
Main var paya raanjha maahi.

Aaj taan roz mubarak charhia, raanjha saade vihde varia,
Hath khoondi modhe kambal dharya, chaakaan vaali shakal banayi,
Aao saiyo ral diyo ni vadhai.

Mukat gaun de vich rullda, jungle joohe de vich rullda.
Hai koi Allah de val bhullda, asal haqiqat khabar na kaai,
Aao saiyo ral diyo ni vadhai.

Bulle Shah ik sauda kita, peeta zahar pyaala peeta.
Na kujh laaha tota leeta, dard dukhaan di gathri chaai,
Aao saiyo ral diyo ni vadhai.
Main var paya raanjha maahi.
