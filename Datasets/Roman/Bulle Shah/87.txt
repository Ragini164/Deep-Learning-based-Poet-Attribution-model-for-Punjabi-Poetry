Main puuchan shah diyaan vaataan ni,
Koi kare asaan naal baataan ni.

Bhulle rahe naam na japia, ghaflat andar yaar hai chhupia,
Oh sidh purakhe tere andar vasia, lagiaan nafas diyaan chaataan ni.
Koi kare asaan naal baataan ni.

Jap lai na ho bholi bhaali, mat tu saaye mukh mukaali,
Ulatti prem nagar di chaali, bhadkan ishq diyaan laataan ni.
Koi kare asaan naal baataan ni.

Bholi na ho, ho siaani, ishq noor da bhar lai paani,
Is duniya di shor kahaani, eh yaar miln diyaan ghaataan ni.
Koi kare asaan naal baataan ni.

Bulla Rab ban baithon aape, tad duniyaan de pe siaape,
Dooti vihda, dushman maape, sab kark pae aan aafaataan ni.
Main puuchan shah diyaan vaataan ni,
Koi kare asaan naal baataan ni.
