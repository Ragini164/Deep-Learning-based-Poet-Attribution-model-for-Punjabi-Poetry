Sach sunde lok na sahinday ne, sach akhiye taan gal painde ne,
Phir sachay paas na bahinday ne, sach mitha aashiq pyare nu.
Chupp karke karein guzare nu.

Sach sharha kare barbadi ae, sach aashiq de ghar shadi ae,
Sach karda navi abadi ae, jihi sharha tareekat haare nu.
Chupp karke karein guzare nu.

Chupp aashiq toh na hundi ae, jis aayi sach sugandhi ae,
Jis malh suhag di gundi ae, chhad duniyan kood pasare nu.
Chupp karke karein guzare nu.

Bulle Shah shauq sach hun bole hain, sach sharha tareekat folle hain,
Gall chauthe pad di kholle hain, jihi sharha tareekat haare nu.
Chupp karke karein guzare nu.
