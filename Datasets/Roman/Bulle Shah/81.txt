Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib di sarkaron,
Dhyaan ki chhajli gyaan ka kooda kaam krodh nit jharoon,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib di sarkaron.

Kaazi jaane hakam jaane faarghati begaron,
Dine raat main eho mangdi door na kar darbaron,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib di sarkaron.

Tuddh bajo mera hor na koi ke val karun pukaaron,
Bullah Shah inayat karke bakra mile deedaron,
Main chuhre-tarri aa sachey sahib di sarkaron.
