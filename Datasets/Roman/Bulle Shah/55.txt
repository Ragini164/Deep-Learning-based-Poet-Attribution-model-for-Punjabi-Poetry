Aape hai tu lahimaaka lahimii,
Aape hai tu niyara,
Gallan sun teriyaan, mera akal giya udd saara,
Sharī'at ton besharī'at karke, bhali khubban vich paayi,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Zara ishq tusada dikhda, parbat kolon bhaara,
Ik ghadi de vekhan kaaran, chukk liya jagg saara,
Kiṭi mehnat mildi nahīn, hun kī kare lukaayi,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Va-vela ki karna, jind jo saare so saare,
Sukhaan da ik pulaa nahīn, dukhaan de khalvaare,
Honi si jo us din hoi, hun ki kariye bhaai,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Salaah na mannda vaat na puchhda, aakh vekhan ki karda,
Kal main kamli te (ajj) oh kamla, hun kyun maitho darda,
Ohle baha ke ramaz chalaayi, dil nu chot lagayi,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Seenae baan dhandala gal vich, is haalat vich jaalaan,
Cha cha sir bhoaye te maara, ro ro yaar sambhaalaan,
Agge vi saeeyaan ne nehu lagaiya, ke main hi preet lagayi,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Jagg vich roshan na tusada, aashiq ton kyun nassde ho,
Vas roso vich bukkal de, apna bhait na dasde ho,
Vichokre vichkaaron phadke, main kar ultee latkaayi,
Jind kudki de muh aayi.

Andar waalaa bahaar aavein, baho pakaṛ khulovaan,
Zahra methon lukkan chhupan, baatan kolay hovaan,
Aise baatan fatti julaiḵhaan, main baatan birlaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Aakhniyaan si aakh sunaayiyan, machla sunnda nahi,
Hath maroraaṇ faṭaaṇ taliyaan, rovaan ḍhaahiṇ ḍhaahiṇ,
Leine thi muṛ dena aaya, ih teri bhallaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Ik ik lahar ajehi aave, nahi dassniyan so dassan,
Sach aakhaan taan sooli faaha, jhooth kahaan taan vassan,
Aisi naazak baat kyun aakhaan, kahindiaan hove paraayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Vahi wasila paakaan da, tusi aape saade hovo,
Jagg vich saan saade jaago, savaan taan naale saunvo,
Jisne tain sang preet lagayi, kihde sukh suvaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Aisian leekaan laayian mainu, hor kai ghar gaale,
Uparvaaron paaven jhaati, vatte phire duaale,
Lukkan chhupan te chhal jaavan, ih teri vadiyaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Tera mera nyaun nabere, roomon kaazi aave,
Khol kitaabaan kare tasalli, dohaan ik bataave,
Bharmaaya kaazi mere utte, main kaazi bharamaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.

Bullah shauh tu keha jeha hun, tu keha main kehi,
Tainu jo main dhoondan lagi, main bhi aap na rahi,
Paaya zahar baatan tainu, baatan andar rushnaayi.
Jind kuḍki de muh aayi.
