Kyun ishq asaan te aaya ae,
Tu aaya hai, main paaya ae.

Ibraheem cha chikha sutaaya, Zakariye sir kalvatr dharaaya,
Yusuf hatto hat vikaya, Kaho saanu ki layaaya ae.
Kyun ishq asaan te aaya ae,
Tu aaya hai, main paaya ae.

Sheikh Sunaano khook charaaya, Shams di khal ult luhaya,
Sooli te Mansoor chadhaaya, Kar hath hun main wal dhaaya ae.
Kyun ishq asaan te aaya ae,
Tu aaya hai, main paaya ae.

Jis ghar vich tera pher hoya, So jal bal koila dheer hoya,
Jad raakh uddi tad seer hoya, Kaho kis gal da sadhraaya ae.
Kyun ishq asaan te aaya ae,
Tu aaya hai, main paaya ae.

Bulle Shah de kaaran kariye, Tan bhatti mann ahiran kariye,
Prem hathauda maarn kariye, Dil loha agg pangharaaya ae.
Kyun ishq asaan te aaya ae,
Tu aaya hai, main paaya ae.
