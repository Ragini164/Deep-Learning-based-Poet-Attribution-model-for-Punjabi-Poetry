Man Atakiyo Shaam Sundar Son,
Kahun Vekhun Baahman Kahun Sheikhah,
Aap Aap Karan Sabh Lekha,
Kia Kia Kholia Hunar Son.

Man Atakiyo Shaam Sundar Son.

Soojh Pari Tab Raam Duhai,
Ham Tum Ek Na Dooja Kaai,
Is Prem Nagar Ke Ghar Son,
Man Atakiyo Shaam Sundar Son.

Pandit Kaun Kit Likh Sunai,
Na Kahi Aaye Na Kahi Jaai,
Jaise Gur Ka Kangana Kar Son.

Man Atakiyo Shaam Sundar Son.

Bullah Shah Di Paireen Pariye,
Sees Kaat Kar Aage Dhariye,
Hun Main Har Dekha Har Har Son.

Man Atakiyo Shaam Sundar Son.
