Main jo tainu akhya koi ghal sunehra,
Chashmaan sej vichayiaan dil kitta dera.
Latk chalenda aavda Shah Inayat mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Udha jo koi hai ja akhe jehra,
Main vich ki takseer hai main barda tera.
Tain bajhon mera kaun hai dil dha na mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Dasta kangan bahein chooriyan gal norang chola,
Maahi mainu kar gaya koi Raaval raunla.
Jal thal aahi maardean dil pathar mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Kya teri maang sandhu bhari sohe ratra chola,
Pai vaang seemeen main kookdi kar dhola dhola.
Anginvein soolan pa laya soolan da khedra,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Main jaata dukh ghar apne dukh pe ghar kaiyan,
Jeha sir sir bhaandar bharda sabh tapdiyan gayian.
Hun aan pai sir apne sab chuk gaya jhera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Jehdiyan saahvare matiyan soi peke hovan,
Shah jinhan de kaail ae chadh seje sovan.
Jis ghar kaunt na boliya soi khali verha,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Main dhoond shahar bhaalya ghallan kasad kihda,
Hun chhadhiyan doli prem di dil dhadke mera.
Dildar Muhammad Arbi hath pakdiyan mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Pehli paudi utri pul-sarate dera,
Haaji Makke jahwan main mukh vekha tera.
A Inayat Qadri dil chahe mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Pehli raat hai saal di dil khofe mera,
Doonghi gor kadendiyan hoyiyan lahdo dera.
Pehla band kholdiyan moun Kaabe mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Mili hai baang Rasool di phul khirda mera,
Sada hoya main hazri hazar tera.
Har pal teri hazari iho sajda mera,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.

Bullah bhadkan Shah layi sine vich bhahi,
Aukha painda prem da so ghatda nahi.
Pe dhakke dil vich jherhe de sir dhai berha,
Main uddikan kar rahi kadi aa kar phera.
