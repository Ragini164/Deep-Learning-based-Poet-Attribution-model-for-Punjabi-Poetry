Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane,
Dil di vedan koi na jaane, Andar des bagaane,
Jis nu chaat Amar di hove, Soi Amar pachhaane,
Ais ishq di oukhi ghaati, Jo chhadhya so jaane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.

Aatish ishq farak tere di, Pal vich saar vikhaayiyan,
Ais ishq de saare kolo, Jag vich diaan duhaaiyan,
Jis tan lage so tan jaane, Dooja koi na jaane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.

Ishq kasaai ne jehi keeti, Rah gayi khabar na kaayi,
Ishq chavatti laayi chaati, Phir na jhaati paayi,
Maapian kolo chhup chhup rovaan, Kar kar lakh bahaane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.

Hijar tere ne jhalli karke, Kamli naam dharaaya,
Sumun bukmoon v umyun ho ke, Apna waqt langhaaya,
Kar hun nazar karam di saayian, Na kar zor dhanaane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.

Has bulaavan tera jaani, Yaad karaan har vele,
Pal pal de vich dard judaai, Tera shaam savere,
Ro ro yaad karaan din raatein, Pichhle waqt vihaane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.

Ishq tera darkaar asaan nu, Har vele har heele,
Paak Rasool Muhammad Saahib, Mere khaas wasile,
Bulleh Shah jo mile pyaara, Lakh karaan shukraane.
Ishq asaan naal keeti, Lok marende taane.
