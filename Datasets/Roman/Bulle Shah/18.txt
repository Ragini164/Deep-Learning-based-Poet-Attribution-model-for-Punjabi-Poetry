Bhaina main katdi katdi hooti,
Pirri piche pachhvaare rahi gayi, hath vich rahi gayi jutti,
Agge charkha piche peeṛha, mere hatho tanda ṭarutti,
Bhaina main katdi katdi hooti.

Daaj javaahar asaan ki karna, jis prem katvaayi mutthi,
Oho chor mera pakad mangaao, jis meri zind kuthi,
Bhaina main katdi katdi hooti.

Bhala ho gaya mera charkha tuta, meri zind azabon chhutti,
Bullah shaoh ne nach nachaye, othe dhum pai kar-kutti,
Bhaina main katdi katdi hooti.
