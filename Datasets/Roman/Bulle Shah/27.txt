Ik has has gall kardeeyaan, ik roondiyan dhondiyan mardiyaan,
Kaho phulli basant bahaar nu, dil loche maahi yaar nu.

Main nhati dhoti rahi gayi, ik gandh maahi dil baha gayi,
Bhaah laaiye haar shingaar nu, dil loche maahi yaar nu.

Main kamli keeti dootiyaan, dukh gher chufaeroan leetiyaan,
Ghar aa maahi deedaar nu, dil loche maahi yaar nu.

Bulle Shah shauh mere ghar aaya, main ghutt raanjhan gal laaya,
Dukh gaye samundar paar nu, dil loche maahi yaar nu.
