Chalo dekhiye us mastanre nu,
Jidhi trinjnaan de vich pai ae dhunm.
Uh te main vahidat vich rangda ae,
Nahi pushta jaat de ki ho tum.

Jehda shor chuphere painda ae,
Uh kol tere nit rahinda ae,
Naale 'Nahan Akbar' kehinda ae,
Naale aakhe 'Wafi-Anfas-Kum'.

Chhad jhooth bharam di basti nu,
Kar ishq di kaim masti nu,
Gaye pahunj sajan di hasti nu,
Jehre ho gaye 'Sum-Bukum-Um'.

Na tera ae na mera ae,
Jag fani jhagda jhera ae,
Bina murshad rahibar kihra ae,
Parh-'Faz-Roohi-Az-Qur-Kum'.

Bulle Shah ih baat ishare di,
Jinna lagi gayi tang nazaare di,
Dass paindi ghar vanjaare di,
Hai 'Yadullah-Fauka-Aidiqum'.

Chalo dekhiye us mastanre nu,
Jidhi trinjnaan de vich pai ae dhunm.
