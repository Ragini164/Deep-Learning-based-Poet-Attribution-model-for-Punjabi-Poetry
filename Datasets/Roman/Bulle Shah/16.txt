Bass kar ji hun bass kar ji,
Ik baat asaan naal hass kar ji.

Tusi dil mere vich vassde ho, aive saathon door kyun nassde ho,
Naale ghutt jaadu dil khassde ho, hun kit wall jaaso nass kar ji.
Bass kar ji hun bass kar ji.

Tusi moian nu maar na mukde si, khiddo vaang khundi nitt kutte si,
Gall kardian da gal ghuttde si, hun teer lagaao kass kar ji.
Bass kar ji hun bass kar ji.

Tusi chhape ho asaan pakre ho, asaan naal zulf de jakhre ho,
Tusi aje chhappan nu takre ho, hun jaan na milda nass kar ji.
Bass kar ji hun bass kar ji.

Bullah shaoh main teri bardi haan, tera mukh vekhan nu mardi haan,
Nitt sau sau minntaan kardi haan, hun baith pinjar vich dhas kar ji.
Bass kar ji hun bass kar ji.
