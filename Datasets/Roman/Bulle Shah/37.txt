Haaji Lok Makke Nu Jaande,
Mera Raanjha Maahi Makka,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.

Main Te Mang Raanjhe Di Hoiya,
Mera Baabal Karda Dhakka,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.

Haaji Lok Makke Vich Jaande,
Mere Ghar Vich Nausho Makka,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.

Vich Haaji Vich Gaji,
Vich Chor Uchakka,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.

Haaji Lok Makke Vich Jaande,
Asaan Jaana Takht Hazaare,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.

Jit Wal Yaar Ute Wal Kaaba,
Bhaavein Phol Kitaaban Chaare,
Ni Main Kamli Haan.
