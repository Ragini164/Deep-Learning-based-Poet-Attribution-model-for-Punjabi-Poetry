Bulle nu samjhavan aayian, Bhainan te bharjaiyan.
Mann lai bulleh saada kehna, Chhad de palla raaiyan.
Aal Nabi Aulad Nabi nu, Tu ki leekan laayian.
Jehda saanu Saiyad sadda, Dozakh milin sajaiyan.
Jo koi saanu raai aakhe, Bahishti peenghan paaiyan.
Raai Saiyan sabhni thaiyan, Rab diyaan beparwahiyan.
Sohniyan parhe hataiyan ne te, Kozhiyan le gal laaiyan.
Je tu lorre baagh bahaaran, Chakkar ho ja raaiyan.
Bullah Shah di zaat ki puchhna ae, Shukar ho raaiyan.
