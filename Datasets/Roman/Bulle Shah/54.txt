Jichar na ishq majazi laage,
Sooi seeve na bin dhaage,
Ishq majazi data hai,
Jis piche mast ho jaata hai.

Ishq jinha di haddi paenda,
Soi nirjeevat mar jaanda,
Ishq pita te maata hai,
Jis piche mast ho jaata hai.

Aashiq da tan sukkda jaaye,
Main khadi chand pir ke saaye,
Vekh mashookan khid khid haase,
Ishq betaal parhaata hai.

Jis te ishq eh aaya hai,
Oh bebas kar dikhaaya hai,
Nasha rom rom mein aaya hai,
Is vich na raati uchla hai.

Har taraf dasenda Maula hai,
Bulleh aashiq vi hun tarda hai,
Jis fikar peea de ghar da hai,
Rabb milda vekh ucharda hai.

Man andar hoeya jhaata hai,
Jis piche mast ho jaata hai.
