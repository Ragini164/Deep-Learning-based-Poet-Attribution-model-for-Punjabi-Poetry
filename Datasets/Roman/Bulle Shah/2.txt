Aao fakiro melay chaliye, arf da sun vaaja re.
Anhad shabad suno bahu rangee, tajjiye bhekh piyaja re.
Anhad baaja sarab milaapi, nirvairi sirnaaja re.
Melay baajo mela autar, rurh giya mool viaaja re.
Kathin fakiri rasta aashiq, kaim karo mann baaja re.
Banda rab brio ik magar sukh, bulha parh jahaan baraaja re.
