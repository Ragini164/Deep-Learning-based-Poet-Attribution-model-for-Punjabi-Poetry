Fad nukta chhod hisabaan nu, kar door kufr diaan babaan nu,
Laah dozakh gor azaabaan nu, kar saaf dile diaan khaabaan nu,
Gall esse ghar vich dhukdi ae, ik nukte vich gall mukdi ae.

Aivein matha zameen ghassaaida, lamma pa mahiraab dikhaai da,
Parh kalma lok hasaai da, dil andar samajh na laai da,
Kadi baat sacchi vi lukdi ae? Ik nukte vich gall mukdi ae.

Kayi haaji ban ban aaye ji, gall neele jaame paaye ji,
Haj vech takke le khaaye ji, bhala ih gall kehnu bhaaye ji,
Kadi baat sacchi bhi lukdi ae? Ik nukte vich gall mukdi ae.

Ik jungle bahirin jaande ni, ik daana roz lai khaande ni,
Be samajh vajood thakaande ni, ghar hovan ho ke maande ni,
Aivein chilliyan vich zind mukdi ae, ik nukte vich gall mukdi ae.

Fad murshad abd khudaai ho, vich masti beparwahi ho,
Bekhaahash benvaahi ho, vich dil de khoob safai ho,
Bulle baat sacchi kadoan rukdi ae? Ik nukte vich gall mukdi ae.
