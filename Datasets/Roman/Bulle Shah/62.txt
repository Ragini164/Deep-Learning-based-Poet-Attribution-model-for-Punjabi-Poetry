Kapoori rewari kyun kar lade patase naal,
Tel tilan de laddoo ne, jalebi pakar mangaai,
Darde nathe kand shakar ton, misri naal ladai,
Kaan lagar nu maaran lage, goche di gall laal.

Ho fariyadi lakhpatiyaan ne, loon te dastak laai,
Gulgulliyan mansooba baddha, papad chot chalaai,
Bhedaan maar palang khapaye, gurgan bura ahivaal.
Kapoori rewari kyun kar lade patase naal.

Gur de laddoo gusse ho ke, perhyaan te fariyadi,
Barfi nu kahe daal chane di, tu hai meri baandi,
Charrhe sahe shehniyan te nachan lage, vaddi pai dhamaal.
Kapoori rewari kyun kar lade patase naal.

Shakar khand kahe misri nu, meri vekh safaai,
Chirve chane ih karn lage, badaane naal ladai,
Chuhiyan kann billi de kutte, ho ho ke khushhaal.
Kapoori rewari kyun kar lade patase naal.

Bullha shah hun kya bataave, jo dase so ladda,
Latt balaati, gut bagaati, koi nahin hath phadda,
Vekho kehi kyaamat aayi, aaya khar Dajjal.
Kapoori rewari kyun kar lade patase naal.
