Ki bedardan sang yaari,
Rovan akhiyan zaaro zaari.

Sanu gaye bedardi chhad ke, hijre sang sine vich gadh ke,
Jismo jind nu lai gaye kadh ke, yeh gall kar gaye hansiyaari.
Ki bedardan sang yaari,

Bedardan da ki bharosa, khauf nahi dil andar maasa,
Chidiyan maut gavaaran haasa, magron hass hass taari maari.
Ki bedardan sang yaari,

Aavan kahi gaye phir na aaye, aavan de sab kaul bhulaaye,
Main bhulli bhull nain lagaaye, kehne mile sanu thag bapaari.
Ki bedardan sang yaari,

Bulle Shah ik sauda kita, peeta zehar pyaala peeta,
Na kuch nafaa na tota leeta, dard dukhaan di gathri bhaari.
Ki bedardan sang yaari,
Rovan akhiyan zaaro zaari.
