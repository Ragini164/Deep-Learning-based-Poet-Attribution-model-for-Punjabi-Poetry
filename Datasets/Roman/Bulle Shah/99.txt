Wah Wah Wahdat Kina Shor, Anhad Bansuri Di Ghungor,
Asan Hun Paya Takht Lahore, Mere Ghar Aaya Piya Hamara.

Jal Gaye Mere Khote Nikhote, Lag Gayi Prem Sachhe Di Chot,
Hun Saanu Os Khassam Di Oat, Mere Ghar Aaya Piya Hamara.

Hun Kia Kanne Saal Vasal, Lag Gaya Mast Piyaala Haath,
Hun Meri Bhul Gayi Zaat Safaat, Mere Ghar Aaya Piya Hamara.

Hun Kia Kiine Bees Pachaas, Preetam Pai Asan Val Jhaat,
Hun Saanu Sabh Jag Disda Laal, Mere Ghar Aaya Piya Hamara.

Hun Saanu Aas Di Faas, Bulla Shah Aaya Hamare Paas,
Sai Puchai Saadi Aas, Mere Ghar Aaya Piya Hamara.
