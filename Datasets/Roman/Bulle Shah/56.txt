Jis tan lagya ishq kamal,
Naache besur te betaal.

Dardmandaan nu koi na chhedhe, aape aapna dukh sahede,
Janmana jeuna mool hugere, aapna boojhe aap khyaal.
Jis tan lagya ishq kamal.

Jis ne ves ishq da ki̱ta, dhur darbaron fatwa li̱ta,
Jadon huzooron piyala pi̱ta, kuch na riha jawaab sawaal.
Jis tan lagya ishq kamal.

Jis de andar vasia yaar, uthiya yaaro yaar pukaar,
Na uh chahe raag na taar, aive baithe khede haal.
Jis tan lagya ishq kamal.

Bullah shauh nagar sach paya, jhootha rola sabh mukaya,
Sachiaan kaaran sach sunaya, paya us da paak jamaal.
Jis tan lagya ishq kamal.
