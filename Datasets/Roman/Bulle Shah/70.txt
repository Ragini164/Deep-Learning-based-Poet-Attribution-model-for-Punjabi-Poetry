Kee jaana main koi,
Ve addiya,
Kee jaana main koi.

Jo koi andar bole chale, Jaat asaadi hoi,
Jis de naal main neho lagaya, O jehi hoi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Chitti chhadar laah sutt kudiye, Pahin phakeera di loyi,
Chitti chhadar nu daagh laggega, Lohi nu daagh na koi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Alif pachhaata be pachhaati, Te talawat hoi,
Seen pachhaata sheen pachhaata, Saadik sabar hoi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Koo koo kardi kumari, Aahi gal vich tauk piyai,
Bas na kardi koo koo kolon, Koo koo andar moyi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Jo kuch karse Allah bhaana, Kuch karse koi,
Jo kuch lekh mathe da likheya, Main us te shukar hoi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Aashiq bakri maashooq qasai, Main main kardi koi,
Jiyun jiyun main main bahuta kardi, Taiun taiun main main koi.
Kee jaana main koi.

Bulle Shah Inayat karke, Shauk sharab ditoi,
Bhala hoyaa asi dooro chhutte, Nede aan ladhoi.
Kee jaana main koi,
Ve addiya,
Kee jaana main koi.
