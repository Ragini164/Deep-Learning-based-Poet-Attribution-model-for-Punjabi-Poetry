Aisa jagiya gyan pleeta.

Na ham Hindu na Turk zaroori, naam ishq di hai manzoori,
Aashiq ne var jeeta, aisa jagiya gyan pleeta.

Vekho thaggaan shor machaya, janmana marna cha banaya.
Moorakh bhule raolla paaya, jis nu aashiq zaahar kita,
Aisa jagiya gyan pleeta.

Bulle aashiq di baat nyaari, prem waliaan badi karaari,
Moorakh di mat aiven maari, vaak sukhna chupp kita,
Aisa jagiya gyan pleeta.
