Khed le vich vihare ghoomi ghoom.

Es vihare vich aala sonhda, ale de vich taaki
Taaki de vich sej vichhavaan, naal piya sang raati
Es vihare de nau darwaze, dasvan gupt rakhati
Os gali di main saar na jaana, jithhe aave piya jaati.

Es vihare vich aala sonhda, ale de vich taaki
Apne piya nu yaad karesan, charke de har phere
Es vihare vich makna haathi, sangal naal kahere
Bulle Shah faqir saeen da, jaagdiyan koon chhere.
Khed le vich vihare ghoomi ghoom.
