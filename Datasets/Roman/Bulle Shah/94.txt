15. Main Vich Main Na Rahi Gayi Rai
Main Vich Main Na Rahi Gayi Rai,
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Jad Vasal Vasal Banayega, Tad Gunge Da Gur Khayega,
Sir Pair Na Apna Payega, Main Ih Hor Na Kise Banayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Hoye Nain Nainan De Barde, Darshan Se Kohan Ton Karde,
Pal Pal Dauran Maare Dardan, Te Koi Lalch Ghat Bharmayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Hun Assan Vahdat Vich Ghar Paya, Vasa Herat De Sang Aaya,
Jeevan Jamman Marn Vanjaya, Apni Sudh Budd Rahi Na Koi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Main Jatta Si Ishq Sukhala, Chahun Nadiyan Da Vehin Ujala,
Kadi Te Agg Bharke Kadi Pala, Nit Birhon Agg Lagayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Dihon Dihon Ishq Nakare Vajde, Ashq Vekh Ute Vall Bhajde,
Tad Tad Tirk Gaye Larh Lajjde, Lag Gaya Nihun Taan Sharam Sidhayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Pyare Bass Kar Bahuti Hoi, Tera Ishq Meri Diljoyi,
Te Bin Mera Sakka Na Koi, Amaan Babal Bhain Na Bhai.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Kadi Ja Asmani Bahinde Ho, Kadi Is Jag Da Dukh Sahinde Ho,
Kadi Pire Mugan Ban Bahinde Ho, Main Taan Ikse Nach Nachayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Tere Hijre Vich Mera Hujra Ae, Dukh Daddha Main Par Guzra Ae,
Kade Ho Mail Mera Mujra Ae, Main Tithon Ghol Ghumayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Tudh Karan Main Aisa Hoeya, Tu Darwaze Band Kar Soeya,
Dar Dasve Te Aan Khiloya, Kade Mann Meri Aashnaai.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.

Bullha Shah Main Tere Ware Haan, Mukh Vekhan De Vanjare Haan,
Kuch Asi Vi Tainu Pyare Haan, Ki Main Aiven Ghol Ghumayi.
Jab Ki Piya Sang Peet Lagayi.
