Ishq hakiki ne mutthi kurhe,
Mainu daso peea da des.

Maapian de ghar baal inhaani, Peet laga luti kurhe,
Mainu daso peea da des.

Mantak maane kannaz kadoori, Main parh parh ilam vaguchi kurhe,
Mainu daso peea da des.

Namaaz roza ohna ki karna, Jinha prem surahi luti kurhe,
Mainu daso peea da des.

Bulleh shaoh di majlis bha ke, Sabh karni meri chhuti kurhe,
Mainu daso peea da des.
