Hoori Khelungi Kahi Bismillah

Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.
Naam Nabi ki ratan chadhi boond pari Allah Allah.
Rang rangeeli ohi khilave, jo sikhi hove fana-fi-Allah,
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.

Alasto se balbikam preetam bole, sabh sakhiyan ne ghunghat kholle,
Kaalu bala hi yun kar bole, La ilaha illallah,
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.

Nahan akrab ki bansi bajayi, man arf-nafsu ki kook sunayi,
Fasumawaju ulha ki dhoom machayi, vich darbar Rasule Allah,
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.

Haath jor kar paon parungi, aziz ho kar benanti karungi,
Jhagda kar bhar jholi loongi, Noor Muhammad salallahu alayhi wasallam.
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.

Fazkurooni ki hoori banaun, fazkurooni pia ko rijhaun,
Aiso pia ke main bal bal jaun, kaisa pia Subhana Allah.
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.

Sibghatullah ki bhar pichkari, Allah us madh pe muh par maari,
Noor Nabi da haq se jaari, Noor Muhammad-salla ilallah,
Bullah Shah di dhoom machi hai, La ilaha illallah.
Hoori khelungi kahi Bismillah.
