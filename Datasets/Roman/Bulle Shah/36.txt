Mere Ghar Vich Chori Hoyi, Sutti Rahi Na Jaagya Koi,
Main Gur Phar Sohji Hoyi, Jo Maal Gya So Tarda Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.

Pehle Maafi Aap Khazana Si, Othe Hairat Hairatkhaana Si,
Phir Vahdat De Vich Aana Si, Kul Juz Da Mujmal Parda Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.

Confusionan Aawaazan Denda, Vahdat Vicho Kasrat Lenda,
Pahin Libas Banda Ban Bahinda, Kar Bandagi Masjid Varda Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.

Roz Misaak Alast Sunaave, Kalubala Azhad Na Chahave,
Vich Kuch Apna Ap Chhupaave, Uh Gin Gin Vastaan Dharda Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.

Gur Allah Aap Kehenda Ae, Gur Ali Nabi Ho Bahinda Ae,
Ghar Har De Dil Vich Rahinda Ae, Uh Khali Bhande Bhar Da Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.

Bulha Shah Nu Ghar Vich Paya, Jis Saangi Saang Banaaya,
Logaan Kolon Bheet Chhupaaya, Uh Dars Parm Da Parhda Ae.
Gur Jo Chaahe So Karda Ae.
