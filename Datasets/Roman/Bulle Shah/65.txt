Kehe laare dena ae saanu do ghadhiyan mil jaai.
Nede vasen thaan na dasen
Dhoonda kit val jaahi,
Aape jhati paai Ahmed
Vekhan taan mur naahi.

Aakh gaiyun mur aaiyo nahi
Sine de vich bhadkan bhahi,
Ikse ghar vich vasdiyaan rasdiyaan
Kit val kook sunai.

Paandhi ja, mera deh suneha
Dil de ule lukda keha,
Naam Allah de na ho veeri
Mukh vekhan nu na tarsai.

Bulle shahu ki laya mainu
Raat adhi hai teri mahima,
Aujarh bele sabh koi darda
So dhoonda main chai chai.
Kehe laare dena ae saanu do ghadhiyan mil jaai.
