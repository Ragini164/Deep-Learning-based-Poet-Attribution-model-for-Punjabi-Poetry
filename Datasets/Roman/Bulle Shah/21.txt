Bullah ki jaana main kaun,
Na main moman vich masitaan, Na main vich kufar diyaan reetaan,
Na main paakaan vich paleetaan, Na main Musa na Pharaoh.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.

Na main andar Bed kitaabaan, Na vich bhangaan na sharaabaan,
Na vich rindaan mast kharaabaan, Na vich jaagan na vich saun.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.

Na vich shaadi na ghamnakaai, Na main vich paleeti paaki,
Na main aabi na main khaaki, Na main aatish na main paun.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.

Na main arabi na Lahori, Na main Hindi shahar Nagauri,
Na Hindu na turak Peshawari, Na main rahinda vich Nadaun.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.

Na main bhait mazhab da paaya, Na main Adam hawwa jaaya,
Na main aapna naam dharaaya, Na vich baithan na vich bhaun.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.

Awal aakhir aap nu jaanaan, Na koi dooja hor pachhaanaan,
Meinthon hor na koi siyaana, Bullah Shah khadha hai kaun.
Bullah ki jaana main kaun.
