Bhave jaan na jaan ve vihrhe aa var mere,
Main tere qurbani ve vihrhe aa var mere.

Tere jeha mainu hor na koi dhunda jungle bela rohi,
Dhunda taan saara jahan ve vihrhe aa var mere.

Lokan de bhaane chaak mahin da raanjha taan lokan vich kehinda,
Saada taan deen imaan ve vihrhe aa var mere.

Maape shor lagi lar tere Shah Inayat Sai mere,
Laayian di laj paal ve vihrhe aa var mere.
Main tere qurbani ve vihrhe aa var mere.
