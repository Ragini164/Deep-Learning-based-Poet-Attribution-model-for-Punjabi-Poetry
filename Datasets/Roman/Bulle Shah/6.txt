Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Aisi aayi mann mein kaai,
Dukh sukh sabh vanjaayo re,
Haar shingaar ko aag lagaun,
Ghat upar dhaandh machaayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Sun ke gyan ki aisi baatan,
Naam nishaan sabhi angaatha,
Koil vaangu kukaa raataan,
Tain aje vi taras na aayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Gall mirgaani sees khapariya,
Bheekh mangna nu ro ro phirya,
Jogan naam bhaya lit dhariya,
Ang bibhoot ramaayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Ishq mulla ne baang dvaayi,
Uth bahudan gal vaajab aayi,
Kar kar sijde ghar val dhaayi,
Mathe mahiraab tikayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Prem nagar de ulte chaale,
Khooni nein hoye khushhaale,
Aape aap fase vich jaale,
Fas fas aap kuhayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Dukh birho na hon puraane,
Jis tan peedan so tan jaane,
Andar jhidkaan baahar taane,
Nehun laggaan dukh paayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Maina maalan rondi pakdi,
Birho pakdi karke takdi,
Ik marna dooji jag di fakdi,
Hun kaun banna ban aayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?

Bulle Shah shauh sang preet lagayi,
Sohni ban than sabh koi aayi,
Vekh ke Shah Inaayat saein,
Jiya mera bhar aayo re;
Ab kyun sajan chir laaio re?
