Aape aahu aape cheeta, aape maaran dhaaya,
Aape sahib aape barda, aape mull vikaya,
Dhola aadmi ban aaya.

Kadi haathi te aswaar hoeya, kadi thootha daang bhuvaya,
Kadi raaval jogi bhogi ho ke, saangi saang banaya,
Dhola aadmi ban aaya.

Baazigar kya baazi kheli, mainu putli vaang nachaya,
Main us padtali nachna haan, jihdi gat mit yaar lakhaya,
Dhola aadmi ban aaya.

Haabil kaabil aadam de jaaye, aadam kis da jaaya,
Bulle Shah unna ton bhi agge aaha, dada god khidaya,
Dhola aadmi ban aaya.

Maula aadmi ban aaya,
Maati vicho hiyra ladha,
Gobar mahin dudh paaya. Rahao.

Sabho uth kataarein badhe,
Dhanne da wagg charaya.
Saadi bhaaji leve nahi,
Bidar de saag aghaya.

Kahun raaval jogi bhogi,
Kahun saangi saang rachaya.
