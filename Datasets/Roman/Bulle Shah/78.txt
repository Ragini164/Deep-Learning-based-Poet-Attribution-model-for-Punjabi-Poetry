Maahi ve tain miliaan sabh dukh hovan door,
Lokan de bhaane chaak chaketa, saada Rabb ghafur.
Maahi ve tain miliaan sabh dukh hovan door.

Milan di khatir chashmaan, bahindiyan si nit jhoor,
Maahi ve tain miliaan sabh dukh hovan door.

Uth gayi hijar judaai jigaron, zahar dikhda noor,
Maahi ve tain miliaan sabh dukh hovan door.

Bullah ramz samajh di paaiya, na neere na door,
Maahi ve tain miliaan sabh dukh hovan door.
