Bansi walia chaka ranjha, tera sur hai sabh nal saanjha,
Teriyaan maujaan saada maanjha, saadi sur te aap milaai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Bansi walia kahan kahavein, sab da nek anoop manaavein,
Akhiyan de vich nazar na aavein, kaisi bikhri khel rachai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Bansi sab koi sune sunaave, arth is da koi virla paave,
Jo koi anhad ki sur paave, so is bansi ka shaudaai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Suniyaan bansi diyaan ghangooraan, kookan tan man vaanghu mooraan,
Dithiyaan is diyaan toraan joraan, ik sur di sabh kala uthai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Is bansi da lanma lekha, jis ne dhoonda tis ne dekha,
Shaadi is bansi di rekha, is vajoodon sifat uthai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Is bansi de panj sat taare, aap apni sur bhare saare,
Ik sur sabh de vich dam maare, saadi is ne hosh bhulai.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.

Bullha puj pe takraar, boohay aan khalote yaar,
Rakhiye kalmay naal byopaar, teri hazrat bhare gawaahi.
Bansi kahan acaraj bajaai.
