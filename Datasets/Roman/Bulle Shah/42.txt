Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida,
Kite mullaan ho bulende ho, kite sunnat farz dasende ho,
Kite Raam duhaai deende ho, kite mathe tilak lagaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Main meri hai ki teri hai, ih ant bhasam di dheeri hai,
Ih dheeri piya ne gheri hai, dheeri nu naach nachaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Kite besir chhorda paauge, kite jor insaan handaauge,
Kite Adam hawa ban aaoge, kadi mainthon vi bhul jaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Baahar zahar dera paaya, aape dhon dhon dhol bajaaya,
Jag te apna aap jitaya, phir Abdul de ghar dhaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Jo bhaal tusadi karda hai, moyaan to agge marda hai,
Oh moyaan vi tithon darda hai, mat moyaan nu maar kuhaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Bindraban mein gaon charaave, Lanka chadh ke naad vajaave,
Makke da ban haji aave, waah waah rang vataaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Mansoor tusan te aaya ae, tusan sooli pakad chadhaaya ae,
Mera bhaai Babal jaaya ae, dio khoon baha mere bhaai da.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Tusi sabhni bhesi theende ho, aape mad aape peende ho,
Mainu har ja tusi dassende ho, aape aap ko aap chukaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Hun paas tusade vaasaangi, na bedil ho ke nasaangi,
Sabh bheth tusade dassangi, kyun mainu ang na laaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Waah jis par karam aveha hai, tahqiq uh vi tehi hai,
Sach sahi ravaayat eha hai, teri nazar mihr tar jaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Vich bhaanbar baagh lavaaida, jehda vicho aap vakhaaida,
Jaan alphon ahad banaaida, taan baatan kya batlaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Beli Allah waali malak ho, tusi aape aapne saalak ho,
Aape khalkat aap khalk ho, aape amar ma'roof karaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Kidhre chor ho kidhre kaazi ho, kite manbar te baha vaazi ho,
Kite tegh bahaadur ghazi ho, aape aapna katka chadhaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Aape Yusuf kaid karaayo, Yunus machhli ton niglaayo,
Sabr keede ghat bahaayo, phir unna takht chadhaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.

Bullah shauh hun sahi sinjaata ho, har soorat naal pachhaata ho,
Kite aate ho kite jaate ho, hun mainthon bhul na jaaida.
Hun kis thein aap chhupaaida.
