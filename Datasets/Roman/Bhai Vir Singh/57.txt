Avanti pure de khandar
Avantipura ki rahi gaya baaki
Do mandran de dher,
Beet chuki sabhyata de khandar
Dasde samay de pher,
Saakhi bhar rahe os akh di
Jis vich motiabind
Hunar pachhanan valon chhaaya
Gun di rahi na jind.
'Josh majhab' te 'kadar-hunar' di
Rahi na thik tameez,
Razi karday horan tain
Aapun bane mariz.
But pooja? 'But' pher ho paye
'Hunar' na partaya hai!
Mar mar ke 'but' pher ugam paye
Gun nu kaun jivaye?
