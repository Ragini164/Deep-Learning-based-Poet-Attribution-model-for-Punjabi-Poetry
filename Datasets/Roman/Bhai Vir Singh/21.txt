Ras jad aawan tanaavan paave,
Dil khicchi lai jaave,
Swaad swaad dil chhadha jaave,
Ang ang ras maave:
Hadd paap di kandh na banna
Jo nigraar dikh aave,
Dil di samajh ik hai jaanu
Jo sabh farq karave.
Oh dil hai hun ras vich matta
Tilakandda jo jaave,
So tilakna da pata na lage
Ras aun mahiv karave:
Ras lainda vich ras de khachda
Tilak malkadde jaave,
Aukhi pal tilakna di eh hai
Jo kaabu nahi aave.
Is pal te jo kaabu paave
Aapa fatah karave,
Aape da kadar ae 'Rasia'
Qudrat raaj kamaave.
