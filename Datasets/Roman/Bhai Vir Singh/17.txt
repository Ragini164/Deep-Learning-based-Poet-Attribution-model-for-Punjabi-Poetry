Ve na russ ke russ ke jaa dhola,
Ave, hasna hasna aa dhola!
Murr aa, murr aa, murr aa dhola!
Gal la, gal la, gal la dhola!
Mun dass na kand dikha dhola,
Kand dass na kand luka dhola!
Larayi di laaj nibah dhola,
Bhullaan saadiyan nah takka dhola!
Dil khas ke nass na jaa dhola,
Khiddo vaang na ae burdka dhola;
Tera maal ae ho chukka dhola,
Apna maal na aap vanna dhola!
Saada vasal da cha ama dhola,
Sajre cha nu daagh na la dhola!
Hath jorke rahiyan mana dhola,
Gal palladu te mun gha dhola!
Murr aa, murr aa, murr aa dhola,
Saanu saatt ke pare na ja dhola!
Bardii teriyaan, ruth na ja dhola,
Baandi teriyaan, truth ke aa dhola!
Pharhia palladu na chhuda dhola,
Payaa vaasta mann manaa dhola!
Kamli ramli de suhaniyan! Aa dhola,
Ho juda na, apni bana dhola!
