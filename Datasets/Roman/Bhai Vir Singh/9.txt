Saanu ramz mili sarkaron,
Ik sainat dhur sarkaron-
Ik sainat dhur darbaron,
Ik bhet dhur darbaron.
Is sainat soji paayi,
Is soji ne hosh guayi,
Ik latak bihoshi di laayi,
Ik matk udari di aayi,
Ik jhoom chhiri rasbhinni,
Ik khiran chhiri rang vanni,
Ik jharn jharn jharnaayi,
Kuch tharar tharar tharaayi,
Jivein thaath-tarab tharaandaa,
Ras bharya ras ban jaanda.
Ve main kamli kamli hoyi,
Jioun tarab kambdi koi.
Koi puchhe taan taar ki bole,
Dil bhet nu taar ki kholle?
Uh te tharar tharar tharaandi,
Ras ho ras bol rasaandi.
Ve jo roop raag da hoye,
Meend vaang o khich khoye.
Panchhi-maarg di ki ve nishaani,
Ras-ratiyan di khoj mukaani.
