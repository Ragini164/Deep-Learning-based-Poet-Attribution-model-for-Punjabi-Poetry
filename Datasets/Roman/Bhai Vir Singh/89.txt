Kashmiran ik lalli akhde
Sai ishq purotee,
Ho bauri nangi pai phirdi
Andron baahron dhotee.
Chanchak kapde pai mange
Aapa pai lukave,
Kise puchhya, 'aj ki hoia?'
Lalli na hoth khulhawe.
'Hun' karke uth gayi suhavi
Mitthi tiori pai,
'Husn-ahsaas' jagian wali
Ramaz kise na pai.
Nagan sundarta nyani nyani
Parda lor na rakhdhi,
Sundarta mutiar jadon ho
Rang roop chadh bhakhdi,
'Husn-ahsaas' jadon 'aape' da
Mad bhar aape takdi,
Aape te aashiq ho aape
'Mad' aape da chakdi,-
Tadon dare mat nazar kise di
Peke mail lagave,
Nazar doosri ton sharmave
'Aapa' pai lukave.
