Preetam-kyon takna ae bulh ve?
Premi-misri mitthi jaan.
Preetam-taan tu chaske maariya
Rasia nahi sujaan.
Preetam-kyon takna ae bulh ve?
Premi-e yaqootan khaan.
Preetam-taan tu laabi lohbiya,
Rasia nahi sujaan.
Premi-main bhora ras-mattia,
Ratta roop chakor,
Ki takan, dass es thaan,
Khadya rang kuch hor?
Preetam-misri na yaqoot ve,
Roop mithaas na jaan,
E taan bulbul bulh ve,
Sangeetik asthaan.
Khullan, mitan ki muskurin
Sangeet lahiri lahraan,
Tharrahat sangeet di
Upje is asthaan.
