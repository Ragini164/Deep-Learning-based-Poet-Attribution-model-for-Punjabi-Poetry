Tera neer nipang
Krishna Ganga tera naaun,
Kaccha sava rang
Dhaai aa rahi.
Suhane Hargobind
Arshon aai jo,
Tin de charan-arbind
Vich tu letdi,-
Hoye 'Krishna Ganga'
Hun tu suhaniye!
Charana da satisang
Kar di unha da.
Agge hor na langh
Charanan kamal ton,
Leen hoi vich rang
Jahu samaai tu!
Preetam di 'Chhuh-ang'
Sajaniye! Je mile,
Phir na chhodiyee sang
Ank samaaviye.
