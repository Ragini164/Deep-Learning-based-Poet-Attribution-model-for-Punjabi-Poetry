Tukri jag ton nyari
Arshan de vich 'kudrat devi'
Sanu 'nazarin' ai,
'Husn-mandal' vich khadi kheddi
Khushiyan chahibar lai.

Dauri ne ik muth bhar liti
Is vich ki ki aiya:-
Parbat, tibbe ate kareve
Vich maidan suhayia,

Chasme, nale, nadian, jhile
Nikke jive samundar,
Thandiyan chhavan, mithiyan havavan,
Ban baghan jive sundar,

Barfa, minha, dhupan te badal
Rutta meve pyare,
Arshi nal nazare aiye
Us muthi vich sare.

Suni ne asman khadoke
Dharti wal taka ke,
Ih muthi khuli te sutiya
Sabh kul jeh taka ke.

Jis thave dharti te ake
Ih muth diggi sari-
Os thau 'Kashmir' ban gia
Tukri jag ton nyari.

Hai dharti par 'chhu asmani'
Sundarta vich lishke,
Dharti de ras, swad, nazare,
'Ramz arsh' di kaske.
