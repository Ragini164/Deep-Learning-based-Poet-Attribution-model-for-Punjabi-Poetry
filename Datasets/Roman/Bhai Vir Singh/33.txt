Inhaan nainan ne maar mukka lia,
Ture jaande nu bannh baha lia,
Bina sanglaan kass kasa lia,
Bina danmaan de barda bana lia,

Kithe arshaan da gia takawna o,
Kithe saai dar nazar jamaawna o,
Nazar melni na naal khakiyaan de,
Tak bannni nooriyan jhaakiyaan te.

Haaye bhul ae nain taka baithe,
Jhaake pahilre khamb khoha baithe!

Jadugari kiti enhaan akhiyan ne,
Maar ghattia sambh ke rakhiyan ne!

Inhaan nainan ne poora de poor laahe,
Jodhe bali variyaam te soor faahe,
Khamb sarre patangiaan vaang bismil,
Pe khich khavan, tadfan hon tilamil.

Main vi maaria ik matkare ne,
Kar keed leeta jaffe takre ne.
Koi karo kaari saadi aan loko!
Koi rab pyaare aa bachaan loko!
Taan asaan vich raahi na shaan loko!
Faathe pinjre aan chhadan loko!
Kare bahurri rab je aap loko!
Viss chadhi da jhadhe ae taap, loko!
Je main apne aap te aas kiti.
Apne bachan di aas phir naas kiti.

Jadugari ne jinhaan nu maaria hai,
Dhuro karamat ne unhaan nu taaria hai.

Tu sataar guffar ae rab saai!
Saante karam kario mehr nazar paai,
Rakh lao saanu enhaan akhiyan ton,
Maaran vaaste saadhaan nu rakhiyan jo,
Bedha karo saada aap paar saai!
Roop, rang, matkiyan taar saai!
