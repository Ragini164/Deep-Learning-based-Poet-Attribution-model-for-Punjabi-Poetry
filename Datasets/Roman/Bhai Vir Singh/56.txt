Kambdi kalaai
Supne vich tusi mile asaanu
Asaan dha galwakkri paayi
Nira noor tusi hath na aaye
Saadi kambdi rahi kalai,

Dha charna te sheesh nivaaya
Saade mathe chhoh na paayi,
Tusi uchche asin neeve saan
Saadi pesh na gayi kai,

Fir larh fadne nu uth daude
Par larh uh 'bijli laheraa'
Uddda jaanda, par uh apni
Chhu saanu gaya laayi:
Mitti chamak pai ih moi
Te tusi luhan vich lishke,
Bijli kund gayi tharraandi
Hun chakachoondh hai chhaayi.
