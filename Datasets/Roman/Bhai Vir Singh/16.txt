Poh mahine de kumlaye te sukhe
Taahli de patte trikhi chall rahi
Pichhon di paun nu:-

Dadhe veg di vagdiye paun bhene!
Saddi dhon kyun bhannadi jaavaniye?
'Palle-maariyan' 'Maun gal laggiyan' nu
Dalon tod kyun bhoen patkavniye?
Tere, dass, ki asan ne maah maarre?
Saddi aahu de aahu pai laahvaniye?
Sai sai karda pata patt digge
Rata mihr na ride tu layavaniye!

Paun da uttar:-

'Chalo chali' di sadd pai goonjdi ae,
Qudrat 'Chalo' di dhoom machaavdiye,
'Turi chalo, nahin thirna kise kidre'
Kook arsh di pai kookanvadiye,
Lakhan vich uddik de khadhe piche
Vaari unhan nu dhakki laavdiye.
Tusi turo age taahli pattio ve!
Pichon fauj pai hor dabavdiye.
