Khade chaman vich aake ditha:
Vanno vanni rang ranga,
Joban bhare phul pe jhooman
Dali patte roop laga.
Tak tak akh mohit hundi,
Man pia penda lobh lubha.
Jafaan paandi paun pyaari
Aa aa lagdi kare suhaan.
Malo malli hath vadhande
Kehnde: 'Laaye phul tura.'
Par jad hath phul nu lagde
Torhdiyan ho jaan suaha.
Jis phul nu cha chhohe ratdi
Chhundian saar jai bhasma.
Phir dekhe boote phal vaale
Naal phalaan de bhare bhara.
Pyaare suhne te manmohne
Dekhdiyan chit lain chura,
Par jad torho raakh honvade,
Achraj, ki achraj is thaan?
Ik purane budhe pipal
Hans keha: 'Na soch kara
'Torhan hukam nahi is jaaga
Dekhan di ik khul bharra!
'Jadugar ik maali isda
Jadoo ditta bagh bana,
'Disse, sohe, man nu mohe,
De taravat naal suhaan,
'Par akhaan da bhog bagh ae,
Bhogan di ih nahi thaan,
'Dekhin te khush hovi veera!
Khadi, tikhi, rang lai jamha,
'Par je 'hath' lain nu pasre
Sundarta jaisi bilma,
'Rakh rahegi mutth andare,
Dil armaan bhare bhar aa.
'Haand haand 'hath' jhaad turenga,
Hare jawaariye vaang bhara.
'Akhe naal pia ras peevi,
Trishna hor visaari cha,
'Tad sabat lahe vich jaise,
Nahi taan jaise aap khunja.'
