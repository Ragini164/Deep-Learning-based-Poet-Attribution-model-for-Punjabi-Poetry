Khade phul nu dhyaani si ik
Dhyaan naal paya bharda,
Asaan puchhya, "Jurre maan! Tu,
Kyun paya is te marda?"
Kehin laga: "Main sunniaan chaahaan
Is de dil diyaan gallan,
Is lai is nu jeeb laan da
Jatan piaan karda."

Yogi dhyaan dharenda hutta
Par phul jeeb na paayi,
'Jeeb-chupp' taan bol rahi si
Jogi nu na disaayi.
Kook kook sohna paya akhe:
"Tan man mera kheda,
Khirdan khidaavan baajo saanu,
Hor surat nahi kaayi."
