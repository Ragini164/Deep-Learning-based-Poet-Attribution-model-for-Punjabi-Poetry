Gyaan agyaan
Kuch jaania akhya jaan leeta,
Koi naaun bi aap bana leeta,
Dhyaan osde vich na rati rahia,
Aale bhul de vich tikka ditta,
Jadon behis hove, hike katha karni,
Tadon gyaan da dhol vaja ditta,
Es gyaan nalohn agyaan changa,
Lagtaar je dhyaan laga leeta.

Kuch jaania ke kuch jaania na,
Naoun dharn di jaach na rati ai,
Kar leya prateet par andarle ne,
Ithe 'asal' hai asal, ik asal bhai!
Es 'asal' 'anant' val lau lagi,
Dhyaan lagaatar es val dhaaiya ee,
Gyaan waar sutto is agyaan utton,
Eh agyaan hi asaan nu bhaaiya ee.
