Trel da tupka
Moti vaangu dalkda
Tupka ih jo trel
Godi baith gulaab di
Has has karda kel;
Vaasi desh arup da
Karda pyaar apaar,
Roopvaan hai ho giya
Pyaari god vichaal.
Arshi kirn ik aavsi,
Laisi es lukai,
Jhoka mat koi paun da
Deve dharti girai.

Nit pyaar khich layanvda
Kare arupo roop;
Arshi preetam hai koi
Nit phir kare arup.
