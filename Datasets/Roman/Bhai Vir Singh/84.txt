Barfan narmi khai hethan utriyan,
Thandak vand vandaai lutaiye apni,
Banke daan-saroop ethe aandiyan,
Nirmal neer anoop hoke vagdiyan.
Thandi toori hawaon dariyon uchheon,
Khedandri e vaao is ton ho raha,
Suhna naal suhao chahibar la raha.

Dharaton kad sir barh chinaran nikliyan,
Chhavan thandiyan tharan hoye khalotiyan,
Thandiyan chhavan jaan minh jio pai raha,
Suhniyan wali shaan ho rahi daan hai.
Thanda thanda neer thandi chhaon hai,
Thandi jhule samir trai e thandiyan,
Mano thandiyan paan tapat mitaan nu.

Tapat hunale vich thanda thau hai,
Bhinni hai khushboi thandak pai rahi.
Jhati kharre pahaad piche pa rahi,
Agge khara madan lori de raha.
Sukh da pir e thau kudrat sohni,
Vaangun pyaari maun balaan vaaste,
Rachya ati ramnik suhavaan vaalra.
