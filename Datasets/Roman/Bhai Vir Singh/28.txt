'Tadap-Gopiyan' Krishna magar jo
Logan pe sunawan,
'Luchhan-Sassi' Pannu piche
Jo thal tadaf dikhawan,
Ranjhe magar heer di ghabar,
Majnu da suk jana,
E nahi 'Moh-Nazare' dikhde,
E koi ramaz chipawan.
He arup! Ih tadap o nahi,
Dhuron tusaan jo laai?
Ki ih ching nahi oh, jehdi
Tusaan sinaan paai?
Milan tusaanu di e lochha,
E hai tadap tusadi,
Jithe ramaz pave koi katki,
E kamli ho jai.
