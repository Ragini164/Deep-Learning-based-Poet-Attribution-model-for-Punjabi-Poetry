Sik sik, ro ro, dhund dhund ke
Majnu umar guaai,
Par pangar na khadhi leli
Dha us paas na aayi.
Ant haar ke baha gaya Majnu
'Leli' 'leli' japda,
Liv leli vich lag gayi andar,
Andar leli aayi.
Leli bhi hun khich khaike,
Majnu labhdi aayi,
'Main leli' leli pai kooke,
Majnu syaan na kaai.
'Main leli' 'main leli' kooke,
Majnu leli hoeya.
Aape preetam ban gaya premi,
Tek jaan andar paai.
