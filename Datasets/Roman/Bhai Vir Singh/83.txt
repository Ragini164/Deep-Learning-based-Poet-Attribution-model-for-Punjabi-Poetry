Pari mahil ton heat urle parbati,
Chashma nikka saaf, hethan jayeshta;
Is ton hethan waar sohne bangle,
Adar kar Kashmir sanu rakhya.

Dal ji de deedar hethan honvade,
Kharre safed saaf jhooman de rahe,
Khetiyan nale bagh safed hil rahe,
Pailan paan chakor, parbat vakhiyan,
'Ban pasuan chihchak' raatein sunindi.
Piche kharre pahaar, nirjan sundarta,
Sha sha da sangit kanni aavda.

Chand chadhe pichhwaar, aage pai rahe,
Parchhaven ramnik suhne sahimve;
Iyun jaape ki shor uhele ho gaya,
Ikant valayat vich aake tik gaye,
Thandi ve samir deendi loria.
