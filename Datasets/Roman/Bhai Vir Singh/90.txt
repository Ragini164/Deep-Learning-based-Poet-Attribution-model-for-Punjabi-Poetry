Wah Nishaat tere farsh suhaawe
Makhmal nu sharmaavan,
Khade khade han phul suhaawe
Suhniyaan vehk lajavaan,
Nehraan vagan, fuvaare chhuttn
Aabshaar jharnaavaan,
Paani kare kallol mastavein
Jhane raag sunaavaan,
Jivein kathak gaavan te nachan
Naal bataava laavaan.

Khade chanaar duvali suhne
Thandian chaavan paavan,
Jivein parkhad Indrapuri ton
Aaye roop dikhaavaan,
Khedde suhaj sundarta saare
Ho kathe rang laavaan.

Par nishaat tere vich kuchh kuchh
Nazar udaasiyaan aavan,
Itna maal husn da paake
Kyun dilgeeriyaan chaavaan?
Sun ih vaak baagh ji roye
Jheeni baani sunaavaan:
Kudrat te maanukh do rachvein
Saaj samaan banaavaan:
Kami nahi main raahi kise gall
Khedde suhaj suhaavaan,
Akhan bhi lakh'an aa aa ke
Meri kadar karaavaan,
Wah wah karn, khirn khush hovan
Ustat meri gaavaan;
Apar nain o noor jahaan de
Jo khichan vich aavaan,
Baure ho aape vich khichde
Naini nain samaavaan,
Nain katore noor-jahaan de
Mad bhar mad bhar lyaavaan,
Nain piyaase apne us de
Nainan vich samaavaan,
Raj raj peen, mast ho nhaavaan
Pyari jhoom jhavaan,
Rang rattre o nain ose chin
Peengh chadhe rang laavaan,
'Husn-ahsaas' apne jaagan
Mad bhar mad barsaavan;
Kade nazar bhar veckhan saanu
Kaamba ik chhadhaavan,
Chhu nainan di ose palak di
Bijli di tharavaan,
Rag reshe saade vich phir ke
Bhar de saundavaan,-
Uh ras bhari lahar si khedda
Uh nishaat mann bhaavan,
Uh deendi ik husn hulaara
Be maloom khir-javaan,
Payar-dor uh nikki jaahi
Sutian tamb jugavaan,
Payar-peer di sohji jis dil
Sai kithe raat lagaavan,
Khedde, khushiyan, phul suhaawe,
Phal, meve pe aavaan,
Dulh dulh pave sunderata aayi;
Hai bahaar te saawan,
Par uh nazar rang vich rati
'Pyar pali' jhakavaan,
Rooh saadi nu kithon aake
Kardi nahi khiraavan.
Is karke ik matk sahmi vi
Be-maloom 'chhu-chhaavan',
Khedde saade de vich vasdi,
Hundi kise lakhavaan,
Payar-peer di sohji jis dil
Sai kithe raat lagaavan.
