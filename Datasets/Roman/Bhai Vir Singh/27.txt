Suhne suhne mehfil asaade
Dekhan aayo sahio!
Ik ton ik chadhande nakashe
Dekh dekh raj rahiyo,
Par ik nakash gupat enna vich
Har nukte vich likheya,
Parhe baad us nakash atalve
Sahio na murh jaayo.
E nakashe nakash rangeela
Jad si jaanda paanda,
Nalo naal ghaib ton koi
Gupat nakash ik vaahda.
Oh si nakash 'vichhoda' sahio!
Asaan nikuteaan padhya,
Kaash! Kade ih nakash mera oh
Zaalim vi parh lainda.
