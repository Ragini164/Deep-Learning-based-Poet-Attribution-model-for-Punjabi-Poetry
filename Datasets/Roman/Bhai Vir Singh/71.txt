Chhambh haarvan lahi lahi karda
Parbat kuchhard chaaya,-
Keeh tu sach much paani hai
Ya naina di ik maya?
Ya ki farsh zamurad utte
Neer balauri aaya?
Ya ik jhund sabz pareeyan da
Vich asmaanan chaaya,-
Neer balauri unhan samjhya
Sheesha kise vichhaaya,
Joban apna umadd umadd vich
Is de chahin takaya,-
Parto sabz unhan da is vich
Denda sawi chaaya?
Ya panne ne pangar khaadi
Baraf vaang dhal aaya?
Panna ho paani hai bharya
Palt apni kaaiya?
Ya parchhaavan hare rang da
Peengh akaashi paaya?
Ya 'Hariaval Kudrat' ne ik
Tain vich raaz chhupaya,
Jia jant nu hare karan da
Eh hai ik kanaaya?
Bhaavein kuch hai neer haarvan
Jad tu nazarain aaya,
Dha saroor akhan vich varia
Aapa jhoom jhumaaya.
