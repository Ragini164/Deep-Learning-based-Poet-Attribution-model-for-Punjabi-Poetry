Vairi Naag Da Pehla Jhalaka
Vairi Naag! Tera pehla jhalaka
Jad akhiyan vich vajda,
Kudrat de kadar da jalwa
Lai lendi ik sijda,
Rang firozi, jhalak balauri,
Dalak motiyan vali
Ruh vich aa ajab hoi
Ji vekh vekh nahi rajda.

Na koi naad sarod suniwe
Phir 'sangeet-ras' chhaaya;
Chupp chaan par roop tire vich
Kavita rang jamaaya,
Sard sard par chhuhiyan tainu
Ruh saroor vich aave,
Gehir gambhir adol suhaave!
Tain kihya jog kamaaya?
