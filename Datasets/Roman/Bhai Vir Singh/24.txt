Mandar suhana, bagh suhava,
Taal thandkan wala.
Sabh kujh ose tarah ose than,
Oho rang rasala,
Khiriya chamman phul han mushke,
Phal phullian bhar aayian.
Vela lapat jalian utte,
Ravshan chhaon suhayian.
Mali, kame, uth, balad pe,
Apne kam karande.
Aadan vagn safaiyan dissan,
Par, jandre dis aande.
Haan, panchi nahi dal kise te,
Shakhan sabhe khali.
Srovar te koi hans na disda,
Phir phir sabh than bhali.
Na koi gala kirtan karda,
Saz na koi bole,
Na mukhra koi katha sunanda,
Na dil takri tole.
Nain bhare ras rang rab de,
Pyaar hanjuaan wale.
Disde nahi Guru de kamre,
Guru Granth de deewale.
Ramzaan wale ya samadhi vich,
Jude nain matwale,
Kise nukre khoje kidhre,
Milan na dhundiyan bhale.
Chhel bandgi wala banka,
Nazar na kidre aave,
Haan, tasveer chhel di latke,
Kandhan pai sajave.
