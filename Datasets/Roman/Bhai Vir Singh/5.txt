Andittha ras-data
Bullha adhkhullian nu, hai
Mere bullha adhmitian nu
Chhuh giya ni, lag giya ni,-
Kaun, kuch la giya?
Swaad ni agammi aaya
Ras jharnat chhiri,
Loon loon lahar utthia
Tan kaamba mitha aa giya.
Hoi haan swaad saari,
Aape ton main aap vaari,-
Aisi rasbhari hoi
Swaad saare dha giya.

Haaye, data disya na
Swaad jinhe ditta esa,
Denda ras-daan data
Aapa kyun luka giya?
