Jinhaan uchyaaiyaan utton
'Buddhi' khanbh saarh dhatthi,
Mallo malli othe dil
Maarda udariyaan;

Pyaale anditthe naal
Bulh lag jaan othe
Ras te saroor chadhe
Jhooman aunan pyaariyaan;

"Gyaani" saanu, hordaa te
"Vahimi dholaa" akhda ae,
"Maare gaye jinhaan laaiaan
Buddhon paar taariyaan!"

"Baithe ve gyaani! Buddhi-
Mandle di kaid vich,
'Valvale de desh' saadiyan
Lag gayian yaariyaan."
