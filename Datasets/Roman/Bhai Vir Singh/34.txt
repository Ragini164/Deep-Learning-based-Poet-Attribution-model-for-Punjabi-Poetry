Guldaoudiyan aaiyan saadiyan
Guldaoudiyan aaiyan!
Ralmil dio vadhaiyan sahio!
Ralmil dio vadhaiyan!
Varha vichhunniyan sahiyan saadiyan,
Jhoom jhumandiyan aaiyan;
Lammiyan lammiyan, saaviyan saaviyan
Gandal kavari jihiyan.
Hasun hasun o kardiyan aaiyan
Aap, hasaandiyan aaiyan,
Khire, khire mathe aaiyan,
Khera naal liaaiyan.
Mukhda mukhda chand chand hai
Chand-mukh ho ke aaiyan,
Khaan-chandaan to chand labhake
Chhere chaarh liaaiyan.
Gagan-mandal ik chand karaney
Keda garab karendaa
Etho vekho dala dala hai
Chand chand chhabi deyndaa!
Ek-ranga oh chand asmaani,
Naal daagh de bharia,
Har-range har raunak etho
Sai lakh chanda charhia.
Guldaoudiyan saadiyan sahio!
Chandar mukho baddh charhian,
Darshan karan chand akashi
Charh dekhe chand lariyan.
Maali akhe: 'Gamle vichon
Ugg utaahan aaiyan',
Par je hethon utte aaiyan,
Chand-mukh kithon laaiyan?
Chand vase asmaani loko
Ambar dor lagaave,
Mitti gamliyan andar nahi
Chand kade luk aave.
'Sita Sita mahima mahi' hai,
Arshi roop vasenda,
Suhjan sundarta vase os than,
Ghadat roop ghadenda.
Suhniyan saadiyan guldaoudiyan,
Otho ho ke aaiyan,
Roop rang ih phabn ajabi,
'Roop-desh' ton laaiyan.
Bhole maali! Gamliyan vichon
Hooraan ih nahi aaiyan,
E apacharaan arsh ton sajna!
Saade ghar utraiyan.
Chere dekh inhan de sajna!
Neejh rang te laavi,
Har jauhar har range dekhi
Gahu kar samajh karavi.
Akhan bhare anand darshano,
Sir saroor jo dhawe,
Sawad rame jo rom-rom vich
Manu mann bhulaave,
Ih ras mitti vich n vasda,
Pattar boote nahi,
Ih ras saadi mehnat nahi,
Dharti ih ras nahi.
Ih ras ghaib khazana, maali!
Ih ras arshi, bhaai!
Ih ras 'Roop-desh' di aabha,
Ih ras dhur ton aai.
Ih ras devanhaariyan sahiyan
Arshon rang laaiyan.
Arshi suhniyan ne aa etho,
Luk luk rasi bhinnaiyan.
- Channan jivein akashon aave
Sheeshiyan te pai damke,
Tive sunderata arshon aave
Suhniyan te pai chamke.
