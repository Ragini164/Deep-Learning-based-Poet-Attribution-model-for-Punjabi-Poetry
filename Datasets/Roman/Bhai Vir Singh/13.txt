Kamal patt te pia haan main haan motee trel,
Jhoonman jee kun neer te pattaa karda kel.
Sooraj risham purotarra haithaan utraya aan,
Sone taar purotarre motee vaangun jaan,
Dalkaan 'Godi-Kamal' main chamkaan te tharraon,
Jee kun khiri saver di kiran de lahiraon.
Bhag bharay jis hath ne palmaya main haith,
Sab nooraan da hath oh kaadar, malak, seth,
Oho suhaava hath hai shaah mere da hath,
Saare hath us hath de rahinde haithaan hath.
Kamal god aj khedda rakhya main us hath,
Kal par godi odi khedanga chhad vith:
Ghalle, sadde paatshaah, aithe othe aap,
Amar khed main odi khed khidave baap.
