Chashma Icchabal Te Doonghian Shaaman
Prashan:

Sanjh hoyi parchhave chhup gaye
Kyun icchabal tu jaari?
Nain sarod kar rahi uve hi
Te turanon vi nahin haari,
Sailani te panchhi maali
Han sabh araam vich aaye,
Sahim swaadla chha riha saare
Te kudrat tik gayi saari.

Chasme da uttar:

Seene khich jinhaan ne khaadi
Oh kar araam nahin bahinde.
Nihun vaale naina ki neendar
Oh dine raat pe vahinde.
Eko lagan lagi lai jaandi
Hai tor anant unhan di,
Vaslon ure mukaam na koi,
So chaal pe nit rahinde.
