Rog ban ke reh gaya pyar shehar da
Main masiha wekhya bemaar tere shehar da
EhdiaN gliaaN meri charhdi jawani kha lai
kiun karaaN na dosta, satgaar tere shehar da
Shehar tere qadar naheeN lokaaN nooN suche pyar di
Raat nooN khulda he har bazaar tere shehar da
Fer manzil waaste ik pair na putya gaya
Es tarah kujh chubhya koi khaar tere shehar da
Jithe moyaaN baad wi kafan naheeN hoya naseeb
Kon pagal hun kare aitbaar tere shehar da
Aithe meri laash tak neelaam kar ditti gai
Lathya qarza na fir wi yaar tere shehar da
