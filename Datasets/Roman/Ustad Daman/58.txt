Ethay inqilaab aavega zaroor

Saade hathaan diyan rekhaan
Pairaan naal metan waalio.
O makhmalaan te reshmaan de
Vich letan waalio.
O do do hatheen daulataan nu
Aj sametan waalio.
O lute puthe hoeya di
Saf lapetan waalio.
Kar laya kothiyan 'ch chaanan,
Kho ke saadi akhian da noor,
Ethay inqilaab aayega zaroor.

Tusi kehri lekhni naal
Saade lekh lekhne chahunde ho
Saade bharaavaan de hathon bhara
Mardian dekhne chahunde ho
Saadiyan haddiyan de bhaanbar
Baldian sekane chahunde ho
Kahnoon hashar toh pehlaan
Phookne shuru kite je tanoor
Ethay inqilaab aayega zaroor.
