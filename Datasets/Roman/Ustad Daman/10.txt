Mainu dass oye Rabb mera,
Main dubbda dubbda jaan.
Main othe dhoondan pyaar nu,
Jithe puttaraan khani maa.

Jithe sahimiyaan rahin jawaniyan,
Te pitda rave niyan.
Jithe kaidi hoiyan bulbulan,
Te baaghi bolan kaan.

Othe phul pe leeraan jaapde,
Te kaliyan khiriyan na.
Main vekhe bakre kusde,
Te le ke tera naam.

Mainu othe cheekaan sundiyan,
Jithe hundi ae chup chaan.
