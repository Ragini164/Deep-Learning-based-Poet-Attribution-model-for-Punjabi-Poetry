Puttar naal mukabla maan da ae,
Belaan beliyan da kihda bhaa panda.
Chitte jhate di kihda hai laaj rakhda,
Kaun maan de sir suaah panda.

Kal nu eh na kehni jahan wale,
Puttar eh kahde apni maan kha gaye.
Ehnaan kolon bacha lai bachian nu,
Kukkad khaan lage saare kaan kha gaye.

Jekar moiaan hai maan de naal utthna,
Jiunde kyun na maan de naal rahiye.
Saanjhi maan ae des de puttran di,
Ehde baaghiyan naal na kyun kahiye.
