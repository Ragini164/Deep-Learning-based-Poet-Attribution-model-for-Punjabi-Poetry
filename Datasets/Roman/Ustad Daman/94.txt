Band band ghulami de naal bajha,
Bandiwaan, band raaz da kholna band.
Hai shah angrez di shaahi andar,
Sach akhna te poora tolna band.

Jazba dil da dil 'ch jazb hoeya,
Hun te hoeya zubaan ton bolna band.
Hai shah angrez di shaahi andar,
Kehna hak te hak tatolna band.

Boln wale te pher vi bolda ne,
Hukam daar da ki chadh ke daar utte.
Jo ghulam azaad khyaal hovan,
Oh azaad ne kul sansaar utte.
