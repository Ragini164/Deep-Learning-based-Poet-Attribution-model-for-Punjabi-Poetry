Tere desh andar diwaaran 'ch laashaan,
Baaghaan 'ch murde, bazaaraan 'ch laashaan,
Kafan to bina nee, hazaaraan 'ch laashaan,
Yeh jiunde jo dikhde kataaraan 'ch laashaan,

Yeh jaanaan jawaanaan, tere desh diyaan,
Yeh shaanaan jawaanaan tere desh diyaan.
Daata hi deve dilavega roti,
Allah da pyaara puchhavega roti,
Bhukhe nu koi khilaavega roti,
Te Maula hi kithon dilavega roti,

Yeh taanaan jawaanaan, tere desh diyaan,
Yeh shaanaan jawaanaan tere desh diyaan.
