Pinjare vich ik tote nu puchhya main,
Teri zindagi vich zindan band ae.
Gaya guzra gaya guzra japna ae,
Teri kise te guzar guzarān band ae.

Tota hasiya, has ke kehni laga,
Achha tera vi kidre dhyan band ae.
Mere jeha ho ke je tu gall kardon,
Main samajhda akal 'ch tan band ae.

Main ghulam haan ik da gall koi na,
Tere lakhān karoṛān da maan band ae.
Khud ghulam te kaiyan nu naal kita,
Tere jeha Hindi Hindostan band ae.

Eh main manna ehdhe vich shak koi na,
Katre pani 'ch ik tufan band ae.
Pinjare vich main piya azad bola,
Tu azad ae te teri zubaan band ae.

'Daman' mard ae vich medaan aa ja,
Karde zahir jo dilli arman band ae.
