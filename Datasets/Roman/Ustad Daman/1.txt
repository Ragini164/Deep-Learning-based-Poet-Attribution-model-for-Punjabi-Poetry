Mainu kaiyan ne akhya, kai vaari
Mainu kaiyan ne akhya kai vaari,
Tu laina Punjabi da naam chhad de.
Godi jidhi 'ch palke jawaan hoiyan,
Uh maan chhad de te graan chhad de.

Je Punjabi, Punjabi ae kookna ain,
Jithe khala khalota uh thaan chhad de.
Mainu injh lagda, lokin akhde ne,
Tu puttran apni maan chhad de.
