Chor vi akhan chor o chor

Choran di pag sadhan laah laayi,
Char chufeere pai gaya shor.
Ethe enni annhi pai gayi,
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.

Chitte kapde saau akhvaande,
Thaggi la laen jaande jaande.
Aisa akhi ghata paande,
Bhijjia itar rumaal sungaande.
A jaave bande nu lor,
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.

Luttan jann bhaarti ban ke,
Vechan maut hayaati ban ke.
Kattan maas eh kaati ban ke,
Dil di takhti kaali kaali,
Chalde ne valiyan di tor.
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.

Phaave ho gaye parh parh munde,
Kuch ban gaye ne chor te gunde.
Kayi bhukhan ne aan ne funde,
Kayi pe vehle phirn lator,
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.

Gandh kapiyan de ethe dere,
Lutt pai gayi chaar chufeere.
Kamban bande shaam savere,
Bhul jaande ghar jaande tor,
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.

Kaale kaale badal aaye,
Badal vekh ke nachan mor.
Kaalian ittan kaale rodd,
Meeh vasa de zoro zor.
Saare ral mil iho aakho,
Chor vi akhan chor o chor.
