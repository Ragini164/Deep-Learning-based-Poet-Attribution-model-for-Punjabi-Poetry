Jithe khushi de vaaje pe vajde san,
Othe hasrataan de daagh daagh rahi gaye.
Kitte zindagi di edaan shaam hoi,
Akhaan rahiyan ki bujhe chiraagh rahi gaye.

Phal lagne san jehde bootiyan nu,
Paani vich oh baagh de baagh rahi gaye.
Unna tabbaraan da riha kuch vi nahi,
Je kuch riha taabahi de daagh rahi gaye.
