Mataan ak ke watte de naal thokaan,
Bahuta hill na charkhe de munniyaan ve.
Tu en sekiyaan phul ke laal hona,
Jadon chhattiya peesia gunghiaan ve.

Ghatte kaudiyan rulegi shaan teri,
Dahri vaang jad ragarh ke munniyaan ve.
Ucchi bolna en beende de vaang 'daaman',
Kise kandh 'ch jaayenga tunniyaan ve.
