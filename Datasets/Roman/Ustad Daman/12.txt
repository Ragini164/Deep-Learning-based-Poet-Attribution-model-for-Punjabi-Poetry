Aahmo saahamne do do hongiyan,
Jekar hashar dihardhe hisaab hoya.
Kehinde zindagi rab di karam bakshish,
Mere vaaste iho azab hoya.

Ude vich ki lahu hai peen joga,
Jehda bhuj ke jigar kabab hoya.
Kuch na kuch taan hona chahida ae,
Ki gunah hoya ki suvaab hoya.

Teri jannat ae odhar, nahi dil aunda,
Jitho pehlo si kabhi jawab hoya.
Khizaan rahe hayati de baagh andar,
Khidh ke dil na kabhi gulaab hoya.

Daulatkhane da mere ki pushe ho,
Daulat lutt gayi khana kharab hoya.
