Lakhan bhes vatta ke vekhe,
Aasan kite jama ke vekhe.
Mithe tilak laga ke vekhe,
Kidhare mon mana ke vekhe.
Uhi raste, uhi painde,
Uho hi haan main challanhaar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Hath kise de auna ki ae?
Mullan ne jatlauna ki ae?
Pandit pale pauna ki ae?
Raat dine bas gallan kar kar,
Kuch nahin ban da aakarkar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Main nahin sikhiya ilm riyazi,
Na main pandit, mullan, kaazi.
Na main daani, na fazzi,
Na main jhagde kar kar raazi.
Na main munshi, aalim fazil,
Na main rind te na hoshiyar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Main nahin khanda Dako Dole,
Rath jeevan nu la hachkole.
Ainve labhda phiraan vichole,
Koi bole te koi na bole.
Mile gile da aadar karke,
Karna apna aap sudhaar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Sabh disde ne vann suvanne,
Kol jaao taan khali chhane.
Dil na manne, te ki manne,
Ainve man manooti kahdi,
Gall na hundi hanne-banne,
Andar khota te baahar sachiyar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Ih duniya ki rola golla,
Koi kehinda ae Maula Maula.
Koi karda ae taal matola,
Koi paounda ae chaal machaula.
Mainu kuch pata nahin chalda,
Ki hunda ae vich sansaar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.

Vali, peer main pagad pagad ke,
Gitte gode ragad ragad ke.
Dil nu hun te jakad jakad ke,
Ainve jhagre jhagad jhagad ke.
Chhad dite ne jhagre jhanje,
Lamme chaudhe khil khilaar.
Mainu paagalpan darkaar.
