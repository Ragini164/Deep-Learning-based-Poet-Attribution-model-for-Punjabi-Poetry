Yaar hunde ne tinn kisam de, vakh vakh pachaan de.
Pehle yaar ne gallan baatein, dooje bhukhe khaan de.
Teje yaar ne jaani mushkil, milda vich jahaan de.
Pehla mile te gallan baatan, khaatir naal zubaan de.

Dooja mile te chheti karda, chaare khaan khuaan de.
Jaani yaar nu aap mili tu, jhagde chhad nukhsaan de.
Lena pave te le dil ohda, deni pave te jaan de.
Phir duniyaan tere lai jannat, aakhan naal imaan de.
