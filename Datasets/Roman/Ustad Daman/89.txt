Chaandi sone te heere di khaan 'te tax.
Baithak diodhi chaubaare daalan 'te tax.

Tax laggiya dukaan te makaan 'te tax.
Baithhe thadhe 'te lag gaya bhalwaan 'te tax.

Akalmand te naale hai naadaan 'te tax.
Paani peen te roti khaan 'te tax.
Aaye gaye musaafir te mehmaan 'te tax.
Anjeel utte tax te Quran 'te tax.

Lag na jaaye deen te imaam 'te tax.
Insaan 'te tax, hawwaan 'te tax.
Doona moe te jiunde di jaan 'te tax.
Is vaaste bolda nahi 'daaman',
Mataan lag jaaye meri zubaan 'te tax.
