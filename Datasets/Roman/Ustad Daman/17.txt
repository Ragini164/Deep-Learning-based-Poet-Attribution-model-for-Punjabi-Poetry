Eh duniyan rurhdi jaandi ae,
Kithe beda banne la Rabba.

Eh raahi raahon ghuse ne,
Aaye baldi de vich buthe ne.
Eh bande tere hi bande ne,
Tu kuch na kuch samjha Rabba.
Eh duniyan rurhdi jaandi ae,
Kithe beda banne la Rabba.

Eh bande haalan khaki ne,
Asmaan di lahande taaki ne.
Dozakh di khohli taaki ne,
Tu aape hi thandi pa Rabba.
Eh duniyan rurhdi jaandi ae,
Kithe beda banne la Rabba.

Jehdi vahti haalaan chhunni si,
Udi ajje kesari chhunni si.
Udi koi muraad na punni si,
Lamban di paindi bhaah Rabba.
Eh duniyan rurhdi jaandi ae,
Kithe beda banne la Rabba.
