Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.
Kujh kehiye pagal kehinde ne,
Chupp raheeye seena sarda ae.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.

Ethay tan ton lehde laahunde ne,
Kar nange jism nachaunde ne.
Izzatan de sir koi kajjda nahi,
Laashaan nu khoob sajaunde ne.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.

Ethay bande vikde dekhe ne,
Ethay haadhe vikde dekhe ne.
Is jag di ultii ganga vich,
Phul dubda patthar tarda ae.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.

Dhan daulat dharam imaan ethay,
Geeta, Anjil, Quran ethay.
Is daulat badle har koi,
Hass hass ke sooli chhad da ae.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.

Ki rishte naate maan ethay,
Hai daulat Ram Rahimaan ethay.
Ethay din nu ghup hanere ne,
Raatan nu sooraj chhad da ae.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.

Ethay leader paan bhulekhe ji,
Ethay lutt de lamme lekhe ji.
Har banda ethay har velle,
Paise da kalma parhda ae.
Kujh kehne nu ji pia karda ae,
Kujh kehne ton vi pia dar da ae.
