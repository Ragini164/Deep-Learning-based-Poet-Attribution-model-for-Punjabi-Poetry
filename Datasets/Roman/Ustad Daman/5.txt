Mere khyaal andar uh, shair shair honda,
Khand, khand nu, zehar nu zehar aakhe.
Jo kuch honda ae hove, na dare hargiz,
Rehim, rehim nu, kahir nu kahir aakhe.

Bhawen hasti di, basti barbaad hove,
Jangal, jangal nu, shehar nu shehar aakhe.
'Daaman' dukhan de bahir 'ch jaaye dubda,
Nadi, nadi nu, nehar nu nehar aakhe.
