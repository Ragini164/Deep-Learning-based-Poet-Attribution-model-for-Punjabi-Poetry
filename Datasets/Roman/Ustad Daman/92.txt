Mahatma ji de falsafe de charche,
Injh ne vich jahan ho gaye.
Topan utte dhondorchhi saanti de,
Kade mantari kade pradhan ho gaye.

Khooh khinj ke mulki riyastan nu,
Aape Asia de nighabaan ho gaye.
Aise zuam andar mere des aaye,
Etho aunde aa ee pareshaan ho gaye.

Aa gayi jaag edar suttian nu,
Utthe, utth ke te sawdhan ho gaye.
Budhe budhe nu nashe javaniyan de,
Te bacche vi sher javan ho gaye.

Ban gaye mujahid te kuch ghazi,
Aayian himmatan maare bhalwan ho gaye.
Vairi vich maidan de thhriya na,
Lalkar maar ke rustam zaman ho gaye.

Sindhi naal Punjabi Baloch saare,
Te Bangaliyan naal Pathan ho gaye.
Panje rale te ik ghasun banya,
Te ghasun to fer ghamsan ho gaye.

Mere des di Pak sarhade utte,
Vekho vairiyan de kabrastan ho gaye.
Kabrastan taan hunde ne momina de,
Bolo Ram de etho shamshan ho gaye.

Edon vad ki mojza hor hona,
'Daaman' jeh dilon musalman ho gaye.
