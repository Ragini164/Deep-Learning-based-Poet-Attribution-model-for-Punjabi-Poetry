18. Kaum de ghaddaro te pukaro kuskaro hun,
Gayi hoyi firangian nu murr ke bulai jao.

Kauman diyan kaumanaan tusin tote tote ki tiyan ne,
Banda banda tote tote hunda te karai jao.

Khai jao khai jao bhet kinhein kholne ne,
Vicho vich khai jao utte rola pai jao.

Bhukhan kolon ho ke tang lokan bangan ditian ne,
Korma pilao raj gharaan 'ch udaai jao.

Charhe paase kabran ne udddi pai hai mitti,
Pakhivasa paida karo kothian banai jao.

Chacha de bhateje nu bhateja deve chaache tain,
Aapo vich vandi jao, aapo vich khai jao.

Annha mare annhi nu ghusun waje thamhi nu,
Jinni annhi pe sake oni annhi pai jao.

Mari diyan chhottiyan 'te chhuttiyan batit kar,
Ghareeban nu Kashmir wali sarak utte pai jao.

Dhidd bharo apne te ehna di hai lor kahdi,
Bhukhiyan nu lambiyan kahaniyan sunai jao.

Band je sharab kiti vaare jaaiye bandshan de,
Ghro-ghri petian te petian puchai jao.

Tola masa rol ke ghareeban di kamai vicho,
Karan te karan America ton mangaai jao.

Khai jao khai jao bhet kinhein kholne ne,
Vicho vich khai jao utte rola pai jao.
