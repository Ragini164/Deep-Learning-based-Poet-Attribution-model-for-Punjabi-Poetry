Ghund mukhre to laah o yaar.

Ghund tere ne anhe kite,
Kai phirde ne chup chupite.
Kinniyan zehar piyale peete,
Kaiyan khoon jigar de peete.
Kaiyan apni khall luhaai,
Kinne charhe ne utte daar.
Ghund mukhre to laah o yaar.

Char diwari ittan di ae,
Burj Atari ittan di ae.
Khed khidari ittan di ae,
Sabh usari ittan di ae.
Ittan de naal itt khadka,
Main main vicho mukka o yaar.
Ghund mukhre to laah o yaar.

Ved kitaaban parh parh thakka,
Kujh na banya kaccha pakka.
Odak rah gaya hakka bakka,
Makke gaya te kujh na mukka.
Aive muk mukaiye kahde,
Mukdi gall mukka o yaar.
Ghund mukhre to laah o yaar.

Masjid mandar tere layi ae,
Bahar andar tere layi ae.
Dil-e-kalander tere layi ae,
Sik ik andar tere layi ae.
Mere kol te dil ee dil ae,
Ude vich sama o yaar.

Ghund mukhre to laah o yaar.
Mukdi gall mukka o yaar.
