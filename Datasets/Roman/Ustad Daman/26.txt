Kade laavein kohinoor 'te deere,
Kade disayi Shahrag ton neere.
Tere ghal-ghuleven mainu,
Paande paaye ne eh kansoa.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Aap kahe tu mainu mano,
Eh patthar ne, patthar bhanno.
Aape tu buttakhane jaake,
Buttan andar karna ae loa.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Do bande tu aap banave,
Phir dohan nu aap sunave.
Tu Momin te eh hai Kafir,
Edon vadh ki hor dhroh.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Ik pandit te ik hai Qazi,
Do rangi te hove raazi.
Saf-saf phir kehinda kyun nahi,
Ehde vich ki hoya loko.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Ih bande jad doven tere,
Phir eh keeh ne here phere.
Ik nu akhein tall vaja lai,
Dooje nu kahe chup khulo.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Kidre masjid vich ghar tera,
Kidre mandir vich basera.
Phirdi tasbeeh mala kidre,
Dohan de vich riha samo.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Teri kul Khudai eh ve,
Phir keeh lutt machai eh ve.
Ik de hathon torre moti,
Dooje nu akhein haar paro.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Jis duniyan de andar rehna ae,
Us de kolon luk luk bahina ae.
Main nahi khadra chhadna tera,
Bhawein has te bhawein ro.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Ih puchhea ae age hora,
Be-zora te keeh Shahzora.
Saade kolon dagh nahi jana,
Is dhonne nu aap hi dho.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Jo kehna ae tanu kehna,
Bin puchhe te main nahi rehna.
Main kehinda haan mann di jebon,
Jo hove so honda ho.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.

Makke puj ke Ganga ja ke,
Gehre kadd te tubbe la ke.
Thakka te saskaya hoya,
Ethe hi 'Daman' gaya khulo.
Tera ik dil ae jaan do.
