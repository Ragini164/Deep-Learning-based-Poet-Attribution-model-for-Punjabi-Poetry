Kal di gall Bukhari piya jiunda si,
Jehdi aaj pe barsi manaunde ne.
Tukkde chun chun udi takreer vaale,
Jhoom jhoom ke saare sunaunde ne.

Baapu sahik moiya baasi tukkrian nu,
Puttar purian daan kraunde ne.
Jiundi jaan rahiyan leera tan utte,
Moiyan baad pe chadar chadhaunde ne.

Jiundi jaan na kothari rahin lai si,
Hun makbare pe banaunde ne.
Jiundi jaan sukh-chain na lain ditta,
Moiyan baad darood puchhaunde ne.

Danishwaraan nu sada khawaar karde,
Bhukha mardaan sooli chadhaunde ne.
Apne kitte te kabhi na sharam aayi,
Dubb maran te pachhtaunde ne.
