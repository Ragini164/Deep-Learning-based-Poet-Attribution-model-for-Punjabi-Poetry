Daulatmandaan de sada ne band boohay
Te gareeban de boohay na baariyan ne.
Mahine baad injh sood da aaye jhakkar,
Jivein maut ne baahaan khilaariyan ne.

Phir hai umar khitaab di lor saanu,
Cha farangiaan ne mattaan maariyaan ne.
Daman kad tak karnge zulm zaalim,
Ik din auniyan saadiyan vaariyan ne.
