Asmaan 'te badal hoye,
Chitte chitte kaale kaale.
Halkay halkay goohray goohray,
Ik dooje de naalo naale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Charhe badal te pende hawal,
Masta haathi golay de gol.
Oh sangal nu tori aonde,
Halo haal te paalo paale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Vadiyaan vadiyaan hikkan vaale,
Motiyan motiyan doliyan vaale.
Eh tille de jogi aaye,
Kaaliyaan kaaliyaan littaan vaale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Inna vich garay bathere,
Inna vich bijli de dere.
Inna vich toofan chhuppe ne,
Aaunde ne kinmain di chaale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Agge e paani chaar chufere,
Utte badal hoye ghaneere.
Haule haule kandhaan kothe,
Halkiyaan halkiyaan chhattan naale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Kujh bande bin nahate dhote,
Gharon nikal ke bahar khalote.
Mein aaya je mein aaya je,
Puttan dharti paan dhamaale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.

Kujh khetan 'te ai jawaani,
Uton rach pai khed asmaani.
Koi chhaara na karda dikhde,
Keeh malik te keeh rakhwale.
Asmaan 'te badal hoye.
