Baccha digda dhahinda ae,
Digge dhinde hi turn da dhang aave.
Sheesha tutda bhajda judda ae,
Edon itt aundi odo sang aave.

Odo zindagi da maza aavda ae,
Faake mastiyaan te jadon rang aave.
Odo junglaan de val mohn karnan,
Dil bastiyaan ton jadon tang aave.

Mera dil husainan de hath aaya,
Jive mundiyan hath patang aave.
Sacha yaar museebat 'ch kam aave,
Aave deep te jive patang aave.

Baazi tang dasti wali khilri ae,
Jehda yaar vi aave badrang aave.
Banda kare ta ki nahi kar sakda,
Mannia vakat vi tang ton tang aave.

Raanjha takht hazario tur te sahi,
Pairan heth siyaalaan da jhang aave.
