Parh parh ilm hazaar kitabaan
 aalim hoye bhaare hu
Harf ik ishaq da parh na jaanan
 bhulle phiran vichaare hu
Ik nigah je aashiq wekhe,
 lakh hazaaran taare hu
Lakh nigah je aalim wekhe,
 kisse na kadhi chahrre hu
Ishq aqal vich manzil bhaari 
saeyaan kohaan de paarre hu
Jinhaan ishq khareed na kita,
 dohen jahanen maare hu
