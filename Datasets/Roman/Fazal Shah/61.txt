Tulla kam de vich mashghool aaha
Aipar kam hesi tatte taa jani
Sade naal piyar de sohni nu
Aake bhandrre ehnaan vakha jani
JIven badlaan thin chann bahar aave
Sohni nikli naal ada jani
Soorat sohni dekh ke sohni di
Izzat baig digga ghash kha jani
Munhon aakh Allah beli pakarr bahon
Laye turt ghulam utha jani
Sohni dekh ke ho hairan khali
Tulla rang nu laye watta jani
Akhin naal ghulam ne das ditta
Ethe koi fareb bana jani
Aakhe peer razaye thin chal geya
Aipar rakheya aap khuda jani
Orrak bhandrre de dikhaye sohni
Izzat baig nu naal le jaa jani
Koi cheez pasand na aundi ae
Kita boht watta satta jani
Mukh dekhne di asal bukh usnu
Hoya aes thin morr murra jani
Kaheya hor thin jaa khareed veera
Sohni hoi ke boht khafa jani
Jadon jhirrak mehboob di kaar hoi
Kahe fer na kujh darha jani
Aipar jhirrak kehi yaaro ssang aahi
Gai jeev te jaan jala jani
Mirze samjheya boht makhol sanda
Orrak laye do chaar chuka jani
Jo kujh mukh thin mangeya sohni ne
Ditta turant palle thin paa jani
Jhal saang piyareyan sajna di
Dere aya haal vanja jani
Lagga zakham avalrra ishq wala
Gai peerr kalejrre dha jani
Yaar vekh ke haal be-haal usda
Kal gal ouchan paas aa jani
Rang zard te haye haye kooken
Eh kehi bani tere bhaa jani
Aakhe peerr kalejrre kaar menu
Koi hazma nahi gaza jani
Jeevan apne ton hath dho betha
Koi waah laave menthe aa jani
Yaaran ro keha jeeve jaan teri
Hune hovasi tudh shifa jani
Sirka khas angoori te arq kai
Rahe sharbat yaar pila jani
Kaaran nabz dikhalne yaar sandi
Laye hikmati kul sada jani
Baldi agg te tail palatteyo ne
Dain kahrreyan naal jala jani
Kujh farq na painda mool yaaro
Rahe zor tabeeb laga jani
Asli marz na koi pachan sake
Aaye naam hakeem rakha jani
Dukh hor te hor dawa kiti
Mukh yaar da asal dawa jani
Ishq mushk doven chupe rehn nahi
Orrak gal gaye yaar paa jani
Mirza ro ke kahe ghulam taain
Chal pher mehboob dikha jani
Aakhe sahiba aj moqoof rakho
Deyo gal nu dilon bhula jani
Mataan samjh jave bap sohni da
Bohte phereyan vich khata jani
Jikar sabar karsen milsi arz tenu
Nit fajar nu jaan tharha jani
Saat is nu saal misaal aahi
Kiven bhalak te kare tika jani
Osse haal andar izzat baig sandi
Gai qeher di raat viha jani
Hoi fajar ghula nu naal leya
Geya ghar tulle kar dha jani
Tulla vich namaz gudaz aaha
Doven ho khale ik daa jani
Jadon pher dua salam mangi
Karan pher sulam dua jani
Subha waqt sohni bahar nikli si
Aftab di ja baja sohni
Paala raat wala is da door kita
Sooraj husn de zor shuaa jani
Geya phul quloob da phul aiven
Ditti aas khuda paja jani
Mukh vekheya dukh te dard sare
Yak baar sun gaye sadha jani
