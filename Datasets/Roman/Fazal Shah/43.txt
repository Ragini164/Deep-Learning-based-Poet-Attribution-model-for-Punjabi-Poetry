Daai vekh ke husn kheyaal kita
Shayed fer yousaf namoodar hoya
Rabb umar bakhshin aes maah taain
Tera fazal be-ant shumar hoya
Daai daye pegham ghulaam taain
Khushi dilaan nu sabar qarar hoya
Geya dorr ghulam khush khabar le ke
Mirze paas ja arz guzar hoya
Sun ke khabar farzand dilbar sandi
Mirza khushi de naal gulzar hoya
Parrhe shukar Alhamd hazar veri
Oganhaar da rab sattar hoya
Kita mangteyan aan hujoom bohta
Bajh tok hath rok vihaar hoya
Ditti meenh de wang barsa dolat
Goya fazal thin abar bahar hoya
