Warrhe chewen harf aese saaf likhe
Karan kul ustad qubool miyan
Warrhe satven nazam ton nasar parrheya
Kita ilm siyaq husool miyan
Warrhe athven sarf te nahav parrheya
Hor phakka hadees rasool miyan
Naaven warrhe kamal hakeem hoya
Kite qaaide kul wasool miyan
Dasven warrhe ata khuda de thin
Parrheya kul faroh asool miyan
Warrhe yaarhven Ilm najoom sanda
Leya samjh sara arz tool miyan
Fazal shah chodhven warrhe taak hoya
Koi ilm na chaddeya mool miyan
