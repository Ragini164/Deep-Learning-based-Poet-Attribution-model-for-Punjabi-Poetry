Le ke khat gai tarf sohni de
Maheenval di mohar laga beli
Chori sass kolon khat yaar wala
Deve yaar de hath pharra beli
Yaro khat keha chann Eid da si
Murr murr jaan nu kare fida beli
Jiyon jiyon paich khole sohni khat waale
Deve jeev de vich gawa beli
Naale kare mutaaleya khat chumme
Rove naal kalejrre laa beli
Agge sajra phatt chamandrra si
Duji waar laya yaar ghaa beli
Taane tez talwar di dhaar kolon
Karde ghaiyal nu ghaiyal chaa beli
Taane yaar wale aahe tez khooni
Gaye mudh kalejrre dhaa beli
Jadon hosh aai ise piyararri ton
Lai qalam dawaat manga beli
Likhe yaar de wal jawab nama
Fazal haal da khat bana beli


(is band de vich eh tak vi likhi mildi he: 
Aakhe eh marham phatt seevne di
Athe peher jo raha dikha beli)
