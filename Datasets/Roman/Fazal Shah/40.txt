Mughal naik saat rujoo aan hoya
Aai phal maqsood di baas miyan
Hath utha dua faqeer mangi
Jaah kam hoi tere raas miyan
Sukki vail teri rab hari karsi
Jaah jeev te rakh dharvas miyan
Tenu rab farzand dilband desi
Naal fazal puchaavsi aas miyan
Aipar likheya vich taqdeer aeven
Thorrhe roz rehsi tere paas miyan
Fazal osnu chodhven saal andar
Karsi ishq kamal udaas miyan
