Sohne dand aahe daane motiyaan de
Sohne tor rakhe paal o paal miyan
Seene saaf te seev vilaaiti sann
Zulfan naag aahe rakhwal miyan
Neem khuwab aahe doven nain usde
Sohna mukh mehtaab misaal miyan
Naal naaz de qadam uthaaundi si
Sohni pair rakhe paal o paal miyan
Sohne pair koole nazuk wang resham
Mehndi naal range laal o laal miyan
Fazal shah jahaan be-jaan hoya
Vekh garam bazar jamaal miyan
