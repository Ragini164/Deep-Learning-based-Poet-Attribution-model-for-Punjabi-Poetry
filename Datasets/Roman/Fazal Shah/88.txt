Ik khas sahelarr sohni di
Puchan haal aai maheenval da ji
Aakhe koi sunehrra de menu
Likh khat saara apne haal da ji
Laai sang dadhar qahaar tenu
Aipar kon taqdeer nu taalda ji
Lai qalam dawat manga kaghaz
Likhe khat te khoon uchalda ji
Maheenval likheya khat yaar walle
Hesi fazal faqeer de naal da ji

(Is band vich eh tuk vi mildi he:
Almaktoob nasfal mulaqat aakhan
Ae waseelrra asal visaal da ji)
