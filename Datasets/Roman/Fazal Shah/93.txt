Bana ke dareya tar ke aauna
Maheenval qadeem de laye jhande
Betha beliyan de maan taan bhai
Takiyadar ho rab te rakh takiya
Duwan paa betha mastaan beli
Ronaq vekh faqeer di lok saare
Mehram haal hoye is de aan beli
Jehrre ghaatt te khas malah aahe
Sonp berriyan gharin oh jaan beli
Nit dain pucha oh faqeer taaiin
Din raat jo gharin pakaan beli
Hot meer shikar shikar karde
Machi bajh shumar beyan beli
Hissa peer faqeer da vakh karke
Maheenval di nazar takaan beli
Aipar sajna bajh hairan is nu
Khaan peen te aish jahaan beli
Nit laa masalrre bhunn machi
Hove yaar de val ravaan beli
Sutte waqt jaan lok daraz theende
Aipar os vele hal paan beli
Odhar sohni bhi intazar kardi
Maheenval di tarf dheyan beli
Pehle gal milde pichon kadh machi
Ral khaan doven jani jaan beli
Gal milan seeti dukh dard jaave
Ghum sool firaq sadhaan beli
Dukh door tmam zrur theende
Jadon rab hunda mehrban beli
Duje bhalak maheenval bhun machi
Aave langh jhanaaon tofan beli
Raatin kaliyan cheer ke yaar kaaran
Karke aunda jaan qurban beli
Mil ke yaar taain pher paar jaave
Bethe apne jaa makaan beli
Fazal shah miyan chori dotiyaan thin
Kai roz is tor langhan beli
