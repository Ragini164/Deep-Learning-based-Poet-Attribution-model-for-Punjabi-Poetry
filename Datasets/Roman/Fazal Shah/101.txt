Mar chuki aan jaan he nakk utte
Methe aa oye beliya wasta ee
Mera aakhri waqt visaal hoya
Gal laa oye beliya wasta ee
Rahe nit chamandrra vich seene
Tera ghaa oye beliya wasta ee
Fauj dard firaq de sool wali
Pai dhaa oye beliya wasta ee
Rom rom andar tera naam aali
Racheya jaa oye beliya wasta ee
Methe fazal di morr muhaar saiyan
Phera paa oye beliya wasta ee
