Koi ilm de bajh na gal karda
Ditta aqal shaoor khuda saain
Kai lakh daana shabaan jehe
Ohde aqal sandi khaak paa saain
Goya fer hoya aflatoon peda
Ditta aaliman hosh bhula saain
Munshi falak de ne hathon qalam sutti
Likhya vekh ke bht safa saain
Jo kujh kahe zuban thin aaliman nu
Koi kare na choon churaan saain
Fazal samjh is nu ilm aqal wala
Hathon liyaaunde adab baja saain
Sabhe darseyan de kite hal is ne
Ditta gal de naal udaa saain
Jehrra ilm thin puche sawal koi
Deve turant jawab suna saain
Kai behes karan aaye misar vichon
Orrak gaye ustad bana saain
Fazal shah te nahi aitbar jis nu
Puche balakh bukhareyon jaa saain
