Taahin sher mera poor seher hoya
Kita rab rasool ata menu
Nahi taan ashiqan da qissa jorrne da
Kikar aunda chain te chaa menu
Dard ishq de naal nibah ditti
Jehrri gal pai gal aa menu
Khadim bhulleya je kite sher andar
Aib dekh taan vi parrda paa menu
Adam bhul geya aidi aqal wala
Koi bajh khata suna menu
Ik bootteyon bagh bana ditta
Maali koi aesa dikhla menu
Akhin khol kareegari vekh meri
Taane maar ne peya jala menu
Fazal shah taain fazal shah kita
Samjh bohat faqeer gada menu
