Warrhe tairhven nu izzat baig taain
Hath teer kaman pharran beli
Teer andaz ustad maidan jaa ke
Nit teer chalaan sikhaan beli
Kol beth jaavan teer andaz saare
Karan qaiyde kul ayaan beli
Sajja pair piche khabba pair agge
Khicho zulf di tarf kamaan beli
Bazu raas karke teer saaf maare
Rakhe vich nishan dheyan beli
Waqt khichan de banh na mool dole
Sah ghuttna jaan pachaan beli
Mirza aqal te ilm da kott aaha
Leya samjh tmam beyan beli
Aese teer tadbeer de naal maare
Cheer khak tode nikal jaan beli
Aaha wang taqdeer ne teer usde
Okha murran bajhon leya jaan beli
Fazal shah aesa teer andaz hoya
Rahe kul ustad hairan beli
