Warrhe panjven vekh ke waar changa
Masjid vich le ja bahaeyo ne
Ik thaal bhar ke suche motiyan da
Ustad di nazar tikaaiyo ne
Neet kher wand sheerni nu
Pehle qaida hath pharriyo ne
Thorrhe dina andar boht piyar seeti
Sara qaida pukht karaiyo ne
Jadon qaida khoob darust hoya
Pher parrhan qurna te laaiyo ne
Alhamd thin shoro kara is nu
Wannas te jaa pachaiyo ne
Sare harf te lafz tehqeeq karke
Kai hafize naal duhraiyo ne
Izzat baig ne yaad quran kita
Sara baap nu yaad sunaiyo ne
Deen boht ustad nu maal dolat
Saropa bitha pehnaiyo ne
Fazal karan ameen izzat baig sandi
Hor shagan shagoon manaiyo ne
