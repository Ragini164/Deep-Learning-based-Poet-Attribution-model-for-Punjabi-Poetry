Aese sohne chel malook doven
Gaye khaak de vich sama miyan
Aipar ishq nu laaj na laaiyo ne
Torr chaarrh gaye nahi laa miyan
Ishq laauna es da naam yaro
Ditta rab rasool puncha miyan
Tu vi aashiq naam dhara betha
Aiven ishq nu paal vakha miyan
Sacha piyar jo yaar de naal paeyo
Karin yaar ton jaan fida miyan
Sir ditteyan sir je hath aave
Sasta vanj he laiin chuka miyan
Main bhi ishq de vich gudaaz hoya
Aipar dasne di nai jaa miyan
Itna dard menu jikar aah maaran
Diyan rukh darakht jala miyan
Munhon boleyan kholeyan dard sara
Hojaavnda yaar khafa miyan
Aipar sabar ditta rabb fazal seeti
Meeti muth hi de langha miyan
