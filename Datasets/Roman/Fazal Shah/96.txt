Pani charrh aya upar kandheyan de
Os raat si aid tofaan miyan
Sohni vich dareya de jaa varri
Kamb gaye ni zameen asmaan miyan
Ambar karrak daraunde radha wangon
Dekh sehem gai ohdi jaan miyan
Murr murr de tasalliyan ji taain
Manda yaar tu mukh bhawaan miyan
Mukh morreyan ishq nu laaj lagge 
Maheenval te jaan qurban miyan
Maheenval he do jahan andar
Maan jaan te deen imaan miyan
Waali rooh da ho udas tureya
Jhalli aa ke waa khizan miyan
Orrak bannh mandasrra thillh pai
Kache gharre utte laya taan miyan
Jo kujh loh mehfooz te likheya si
Soi woh milsi aj aan miyan
Kare jaan havalrre yaar de ji
Jehrra yaar si vird zuban miyan
Aj shakh umeed di tutt pessi
Hosi bagh vujood veraan miyan
Fazal yaar ton mukh na morreyo su
Hoi yaar de val ravaan miyan
