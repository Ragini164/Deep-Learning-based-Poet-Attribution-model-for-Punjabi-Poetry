Sohni kookadi beliya beliya o
Das kon dardi aede hain tere
Ik saaha aaha tera naal mere
Ethe hor nahi saak sain tere
Picha door raha tera sajna o
Hun kol nahi bhai bhen tere
Phirsen kandheyan te daavan dolaan bhonda
Koi na sun si sunje vayn tere
Jadon dard firaq di dhaarr pesi
Kehrre val vesan sukh chain tere
Fazal naal shala jagaan teek jiven
Khushi naal guzran din rain tere
