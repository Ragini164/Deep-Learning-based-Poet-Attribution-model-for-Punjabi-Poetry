Moonhon aakhdi tattiye sohniye ni
Assin tatteyan nu ditto taa dhiya
Aese waste paaleyon poseyon tu
Izzat baap di daen gawa dhiya
Ni tu laj asadarri laah sutti
Changa kito ee haq ada dhiya
Hun waqt na aunda hath mere
Dendi jamdeyan zeher pila dhiya
Kidhar sohniye! geya hayaao tera
Waari akhiyan nu samjha dhiya
Phiren watdi katdi mool nahi
Kharri rahen mehel te jaa dhiya
Samjh jaa je zindagi lorrni ain
Jale dilaan nu na jala dhiya
Shala maut aavi ki kujh samjh kito
Ishq kaameyan naal rava dhiya
Shala maren tu dariye kavariye ni
Ho jaa door na pai khapa dhiya
Fazal maan aakhe tethon boht hoi
Assin raj rahe jaan jaa dhiya
