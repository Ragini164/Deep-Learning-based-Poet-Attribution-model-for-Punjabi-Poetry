Maheenval kandhe utte kookda si
Keha laa geyon menu baal beli
Izraail ne mujh vanjararre di
Lutt lai he kul dukaan beli
Tudh yaar di qasam na jooth mole
Tere bajh jahan veraan beli
Lad geyon be-khabar be-sabar kolon
Saang mareyo jaan pachaan beli
Aiven chup chaptrra nass geyon
Koi dass na thaan makaan beli
Lak torr geyon assan aajizan da
Sunji maut da maar vidaan beli
Aave sabar nahi akhin rondeyan nu
Menu deh dalasrra aan beli
Hukum rab de thin laash sohni di
Hoi yaar de val ravaan beli
Jidhar yaar aya odhar lash gai
Pani laa raha lakh taann beli
Oven nain aahe intzar doven
Maheenval di tarf dheyan beli
Rurrhdi laash aai nerre kandhrre de
Jithe khala aaha prehsan beli
Dekho ishq eh aashiqan saadkan da
Butt qudraton kare beyan beli
Doven hath pisaar pukar kiti
Mil jaah menu mere haan beli
Uchanchet aaha dooron nazar peya
Jinhaan beliyan da nigehbaan beli
Maheenval maare chaal vich nain de
Moonhon aakhda main qurban beli
Gal lag mile bujhu agg dil di
Maheenval ditti oven jaan beli
Sooraj wang gharoob hogaye dove
Labhe jaa kite doonghi thaan beli
Piche ishq jaani ditti jaan jaani
Rakh sidq yaqeen imaan beli
Bijli aah fikar de naal maare
Puhnchi khabar zameen asmaan beli
Uchi rovan laga badal maar cheekan
Suni aah faghaan jahaan beli
Bhaaven roz qeyamtan aan puhncha
Kita kul jahaan gumaan beli
Jaan ditteyan bajh na yaar milda
Jaan bujh ke jaan vanjaan beli
Ditti jaan te jaan nu paaleya
Bajhon jaan de jaan na jaan beli
Fazal shah par khatima aashiqan da
Karo kul beyaan ayaan beli
