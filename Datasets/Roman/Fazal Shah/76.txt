Aiven paarr na pai asmaan taain
Aje sikhni hain wall yaariyan de
Piche chaak de maran qaboolni hain
Sharmaan sohniye kul utaariyan ni
Tu taan laj asadrri laah sutti
Mangan khasam na dhiyaan kavariyan ni
Aap jansen gi jadon tudh taain
Pain aan khuwariyan bhariyan ni
Menu sohniye na parwah aahi
Aipar waah peya naal dariyaan ni
Fazal shah de naal piyar paaiyo
Dob satteyo ni wahiyan saariyan ni
