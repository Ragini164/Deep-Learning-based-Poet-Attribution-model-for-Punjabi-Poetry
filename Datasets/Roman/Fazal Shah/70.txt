Aide makar fareb bana dhiya
Kehrri gal utton menu mat ditti
Kehrri gal nu pakrreyo jaa dhiya
Aali bholrri kujh na jaandi he
Tethin gal da wal na aa dhiya
Yaari laaiyo ee naal kaameyan de
Vekhaan ki hois tere bha dhiya
Chadar satar haya di chaak karke
Piche chaak phiren mubtala dhiya
