Teri gal tamam main janani haan
Gallan naal na pai wasah dhiya
Hensariye kavariye daariye ni!
Dhiyan betiyan de karin raah dhiya
Pai mukh sawaar ke karen gallan
Methon aesiyan sikh ke jaan dhiya
Bhath peya tera nij jamna ni
Pat sutteyo ee sadi laah dhiya
Ni tu khavand chaheya lorrni hain
Aj kal karsaan tera viyah dhiya
Fazal shah di qasam na jhooth molon
Menu sohniye laya ee daah dhiya
