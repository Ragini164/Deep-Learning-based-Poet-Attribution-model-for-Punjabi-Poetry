Maai charrh chobare te kookadi si
Mera vekh akhin sanda neer bacha
Be-tarseya tars na mool tenu
Menu gaiyyon bana faqeer bacha
Laai sang firaq di kaar menu
Gai haan kaljrre cheer bacha
Aes dard andar marjaavsan gi
Tenu bakhsheya apna apn sheer bacha
Khaan peen aram haram hoya
Kaheya laa geya menu teer bacha
Jo kujh loh mehfooz thin likheya si
Soi woh mili taqdeer bacha
Aakhe lag lokaan henseyareyan ton
Tareyon shonq di torr zanjeer bacha
Nabi pak rasool di rakh hovi
Nigehbaan tere panj peer miyan
Jithon teek nigah daraz aahi
Rahi vekhadi laa-nazeer bacha
Ve main rab raheem nu sonpeyon tu
Jis ne maari aan be-taqseer miyan
Jadon dasnon raha farzand isnu
Diggi aakh ke "main dilgeer bacha"
Naal fazal de pher milap sada
Jadon hovasi roz akheer bacha
