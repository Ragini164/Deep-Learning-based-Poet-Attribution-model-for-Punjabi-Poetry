Orrak maan nu aakheya sohni ne
Jame mujh na kujh khata maai
Ishq ishq dassen war war menu
Tere ishq di khabar na kaa maai
Be-taqseer taain bura bolni ain
Naale deni hain buri dua maai
Piyari dhee nu choriyan laauni ain
Bhaven hor ne leya chura maai
Ki kujh rang aaha? kitne qad hesi?
Kis de vich ton rakheya paa maai
Shala hath sarre oh jo ishq tera
Je main khol dittha hath laa maai
Yaa oh khaan aali teri cheez aahi
Ya oh wartane de vich aa maai
Naal kaameyan de kikar laya main
Eh vi khol ke haal suna maai
Menu ambrriye ki parwah hesi
Kardi aid luka chupa maai
Lendi rubaru je menu lorr hundi
Na si tudh thin kujh luka maai
Tu beyojrreyan nu jhootthe uzar deven
Uchi boleyon dhum to paa maai
Vedan apni jeev di das menu
Bhaven bethi hain kujh tu kha maai
Agge injh tu kade na mool kita
Jiven aj kiti mere bha maai
Jikar marz shuda da asar hoya
Fazal shah nu nabz vakha maai
