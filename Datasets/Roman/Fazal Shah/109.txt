Bhora rooh da ho udas tureya
Lamme vain paa ke Allah aaneyan nu
Jande rooh nu butt pukar kiti
Kithe chalaen chad nimaneyan nu
Manu bani te hor banaiyo ne
Badhe bhar o yaar pilaniyan nu
Sohni vich jhanaon de dob chaleyon
Mach kach sansaar de khaneyan nu
Jandi waar na kiti aa gal koi
Picha ditta ee umar vihaniyan nu
Adhi raat karvaniyan kooch kita
Chad challeyon tanbuwan taneyan nu
Vich sheh dareya de dob challeyon
Sabh maal mata pilaniyan nu
Pehle yaar banaiyo piyar seeti
Pichon mareyo kul siyaneyan nu
Wang baz de tez parvaz kito
Raz dasseyo na anjaneyan nu
Naam rab de mann sawal mera
Gal laa ik war nimaneyan nu
Dob sohni nu kehrri wal vesen
Yara das ke jaah tikaneyan nu
Nahi kam aseel da chad dena
Yaaran beliyan boht puraneyan nu
Kahnu jaduvrra paa ke thageyo ee
Maheenval ton ghol ghumaniyan nu
Kehnu sonp challeyon vich nain de oye
Assan ajizan dard ranjaneyan nu
Nahi naal pardesiyan bhul laya
Khet beejeya bhunneyan daaneyan nu
Fazal shah miyan aiven lekh aahe
Morre kon khuda de bhaneyan nu
