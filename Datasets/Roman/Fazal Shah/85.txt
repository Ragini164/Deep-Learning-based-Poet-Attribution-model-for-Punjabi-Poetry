Doven dhi nu kite mangavne di
Ise roz salah pakaunde ji
Ik vich gujrat ghumeyar aaha
Putar is de naal mangaunde ji
Karke daaj da kaaj teyar karde
Kujh roz paake ghandin paunde ji
Sundar chel malook te hor baanke
Jorr mail ke janj leyaunde ji
Orrak sohni rondarri dhondrri nu
Doli paai viyah le jaaunde ji
Qissa viyah da tool na mool kita
Parrhan walrre chit na laaunde ji
Gal dard de naal he gharz menu
Kita tool na jiven vadhaunde ji
Fazal shah tanandrra sher keha
Jehrre she be-dard alaaunde ji
