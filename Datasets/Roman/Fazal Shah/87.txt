Maheenval nu zameen na vehl deve
Sohni viyah le gaye jaan hor beli
Jehrre maal piche maheenval baneya
Soi ghin gaye sunje chor beli
Agge dukh aaha vicharr jaan wala
Paya aan dukhaan hor zor beli
Sunje kaag ne aan makan kita
Jehrre bagh sann hans te mor beli
Meri aas murad na mool punni
Rwha peer faqeer nu shor beli
Phul laa muhabtan beejeya si
Na si khabar jo hoseya shor beli
Nikla jaan vesi kise roz meri
Kardi aah faghaan te shor beli
Fazal bhath peya mera jeevna oye
Tudh bajh menu changi gor beli
