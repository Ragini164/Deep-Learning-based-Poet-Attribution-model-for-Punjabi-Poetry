Yousaf naal firaq da aah mari
Veh mili tenu taqdeer beli
Pash pash zulekha da jeev hoya
Jehrri ashiqan de vich peer beli
Majnu banh ulaar pukar kiti
Bahin bhann geyon mere veer beli
Laila le lai dukh dard gham ne
Paya faiz firaq vaheer beli
Farha farhad fareyad kiti
Laga kaar kalejrre teer beli
Kaurre guhar gham de sheerin ghutt bhardi
Dulh peya akhin sunda neer beli
Ranjhe sanda seena chaak hoya
Naale kharri rove kol heer beli
Machi wang peya punnu tarrfda si
Ditta khak rala sareer beli
Khullhe waal dullhe nain vain kardi
Sassi vehshiyan di tasveer beli
Mirza kookda si haye haye yaro
Menu gaye banaye faqeer beli
Runni sahiban bhi uchi maar cheekan
Mahi yaar kooke yaar yaar beli
Kita dard firaq zaheer beli
Chandan badan ne khak rala ditta
Sohna badan jo maah muneer beli
Fazal hor karorr jo hujoom aaha
Nahin vich tehreer taqreer beli
