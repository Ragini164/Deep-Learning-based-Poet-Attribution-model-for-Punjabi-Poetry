Odhar sohni rondarri beth goshe
Dass jaa menu bhala beliya oye
Aakhe dotiyan de yaar chad nahi
Gal laa menu bhala beliyan oye
Tujh bajh aram haram hoya
Tera chaa menu bhala beliya oye
sunje dard be-dard ne kard laa ke
Katha cha menu bhala beliyan oye
Jichar milen an sabar qarar aave
Kise daa menu bhala beliya oye
Munh de bhar paiyaan kutthi dard gham di
Aa cha menu bhala beliya oye
Naam rab de karin kheyal saaiyan 
Mukh vakha menu bhala beliya oye
Seene saang maare vicharr jaan tera
Ghera paa menu bhala beliya oye
Tujh bajh jahan de dukh saare
Paye dhaa menu bhala beliya oye
Fazal kujh na sujhda mujh taain
