Awwal hamd hazar khuda taain
Jindi sift da ant na kaa jaani
Lakh waar darood hazoor taain
Jis di shaam "lolak" kalma jaani
Is thin baad karorr salam puche
Suni haal tu jabaja jaani
Jis roz maai baap tudh taain
Mahin charnon dain hata jaani
Aeven vavela pai kookadi saan
Methe aa jani methe aa jani
Bina maut aai koi mare nahi
Bethi khaan te peen chuka jaani
Meheenval mera waal waal kooke
Sunji maan nu rahiyan kurlaa jaani
Orrak mujh mangaye viyaheo ne
Zor o zor ditti doli paa jaani
Gana torr ke sutteya vich vehrre
Ditte kaprre khak rala jaani
Jikar khabar hundi ehnan bannh deni
Mardi khaai ke kujh hala jaani
Aipar eh sharmindagi likhi aahi
Tudh yaar wallon mere bhaa janai
Ghar saahre mool na gal kiti
Sunji sass bhi rahi bala jaani
Mere paas raqeeb sawaleyo ne
Sunji palang te sej vacha jaani
Lagga dast daraz raqeeb karne
Himmat rab ditti menu chaa jaani
Sari umar mere hath yaad karsi
Ditti maar ke hosh bhala jaani
Maheenval di rakh amaan rabba
Mangi dua main hath uthaa jaani
Meri arsh azeem te kook puhnchi 
Pai turant qabool dua jaani
Oven rab de karam te fazal kolon
Sutta mujh ton ho juda jaani
Soven nit navekla mujh kolon
Kadi nanh vekh akhin chaa jaani
Ghar sohre khas anjorr mere
Ditti pekeyan manu bhula jaani
Saaat saal menu akhin neend nahi
Kakkar qeher di rain veha jaani
Bethi yaad karaan mere saaiyan o
Tera cha jaani tera chaa jani
Tujh bajh palangh palangh disse
Sej seekh te jaan charrha jaani
Hayaan mang sheehan karrak paye menu
Ik palak na mile taka jaani
Davan khaavan aaundi mujh taai
Jiven dang maare aidhaa jaani
Paave vekhne thin dhaave sool menu
Aave dukh paave ghabra jaani
Lef saif maare jikar loovan utte
Dukh sool wala deve taa jaani
Jo kujh haal mera mere saaiyan o
Ditta tudh nu sabh suna jaani
Baldi agg te tai; palatteyo ee
Ditto bharrkdi nu bharrka jaani
Taane likheyo ne aatish wang tatte
Gaye jigar ta jaan jala jaani
Laavan bhaah nakhsmrre khasam taain
Jaata sidq thin ik khuda jaani
Aape vekhsen kufal sandooq jorre
Baldi tail na paa macha janai
Taqal nanh jo jooth nu jooth aakhan
Jo kujh likheya naal zara jaani
Is qol upar pehra devsaan gi
Maraan karaan je pher phara jaani
Jikar tudh thin piyareya mukh morran
Shala dozkhin mile saza jaani
Shahid rab mera, tuhin khas kaba
Sajda hor na kite rava jaani
Laiyaan naal tere laavan piyareya o
Roz azal de aqad parrha jaani
Daman laggiyan di rakhin sharam saaiyan
Damaan bajh main rahi vikaa jaani
Main te goliyan di pargolrri aan
Naal sidq yaqeen safa jaani
Oho naal imaan aman teri
Rakhi gher thin bohat chupa jaani
Kadi aab e hayaat visaal wala
Assan piyaseyan nu aa pila jaani
Kise naal bahanrre aa ethe
Kiven piyareya mukh dakha jaani
Doti koi pachaan na mool sake
Koi aavna bhes vatta jaani
Oganhaar bemar lachar pai aan
Tera dekhna asal dawa jaani
Naam rab de milen piyareya o
Moi pai nu pher jiva jaani
Karsaan hor salah main naal tere
Jadon aavsen ga mere daa jaani
Murr ke sohni da khat yaar walle
Le ke geya shataab sadha jaani
Fazal yaar wala khat yaar taain
Ditta os ne pher pacha jaani
