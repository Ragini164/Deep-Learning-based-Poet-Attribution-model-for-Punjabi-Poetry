Beth jeev de naal salah kiti
Paya dostan jadon sawal miyan
Qisse ishq de nu naal hosh kehna
Khara rakhna pair sambhal miyan
Ishq vich museebtan bht mushkil
Banda kon jhalle ohdi jhal miyan
Lakhan peer wali ishq dob ditte
Gal paa prem da jaal miyan
Ishq aqal bhula khawa dana
Adam jannaton de nikaal miyan
Ibrahim taain es ishq zalim
Ditta chakha namrood di daal miyan
Yousaf ibn e yaqoob nu khuh paya
Es sihq ne khuwab dikhaal miyan
Muthi khuwab de vich taimoos beti
Soorat yousaf di dekh kamal miyan
Zalim ishq ne maar shaheed kita
Bibi Fatima de doven laal miyan
Dhol tome te shaam de shah taain
Ishq maar litte dhol dhaal miyan
