Mere milan sandi jeekar sik tenu
Dassan apna thaan makaan beli
Navaan kott lahore di tarf dakhan
Penda neem phir sang da jaan beli
Os jaga te main wasnaik kiti
Gal ishq di kul beyaan beli
Veehven saal andar mera peer aaha
Kita khizar menu kher daan beli
Kiti jorr kitaab darust saari
Roze chodhven maah ramazan beli
Hijrat nabi kareem thin gaye aahe
Baarhan sae te penth pachaan beli
Akhin khol ke vekh peyareyan de
Kita ishq da beher ravaan beli
Jikar ishq thin mile maqsood tera
Methin karen to dard ayaan beli
Acha jaan nahi jinhaan ishq nahi
Tinhaan mool na deen imaan beli
Daman ishq de laggeyan yaar milda
Laiin samjh na theyo nadaan beli
Main bhi ishq da palrra pakrreya si
Mileya yaar mera jeev jaan beli
Fazal shah sandi hoi ishq wallon
Kalma nabi de naal zuban beli
