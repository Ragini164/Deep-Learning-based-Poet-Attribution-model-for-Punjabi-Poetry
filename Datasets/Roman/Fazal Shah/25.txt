Warrhe baarhven vich parivaar sohni
Jiven chodhvin chand parvar beli
Tarinjan vich bethi saiyaan naal sohni
Pain charkheyan de ghumkaar beli
Koi daye jotte koi daye pooni
Koi katdi baanh ulaar beli
Koi male dandasrra koi surkhi
Koi laaundi haar shinghar beli
Koi paa surma koi paa kajjal
Nokdaar rakhe kaali dhaar beli
Koi husn te ho maghroor bethi
Koi gal kardi naal piyar beli
Koi tez nigah de naal vekhe
Tikhe nain jiven taiz kattar beli
Koi phaahne waste aashiqan de
Paaye zulfan de pech sawaar beli
Saiyaan ik ton ik charrhendiyan sann
Aipar sohni si sardar beli
Fazal shah tareef hun sohni di
Assin aakhde haan mazedar beli
