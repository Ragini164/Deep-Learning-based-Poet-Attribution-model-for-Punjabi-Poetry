Tera haal ehwaal maloom menu
Bas roh de vich na aa dhiya
Jikar maut aave tere jahiyan nu
Maape leyaaunde shukar baja dhiya
Eh bhi makar fareb kamal tera
Jehrra rovan utte paiyo taa dhiya
Tudh jahiyan na daran balaiyaan ton
Raatin cheer vanjan dareya dhiya
Aivan jaanni hain main bhi maan teri
Menu nain na pai dikha dhiya 
Raatin baap tere taain khabar karsaan
Jindon deseya tudh vanjaa dhiya
Tenu jaandi haan kaare hathiye ni!
Gallan naal na pai thaga dhiya
Maheenval nu cha jawab desaan
Jadon leyavsi mahin chura dhiya
Ang saak qabeelrra ki saada
Kehrre desh di oh bala dhiya
Fazal rab de thin kal leh vesi
Maheenval wala tera chaa dhiya

(Is band vich eh tuk vi likhi mildi he:
Chor chatur wangon litar litar karen
Sare satar haya gawa dhiya )
