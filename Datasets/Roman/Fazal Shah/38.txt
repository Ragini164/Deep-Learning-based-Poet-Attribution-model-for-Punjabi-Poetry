Aesi baat kolon preshan hoya
Gaye aesh jahaan de bhool yara
Kise naal na hass ke gal karda
Rahe ronda nit malool yara
Ghamaan ghor chofer andher kita
Lain den araam na mool yara
Kise aai razaye thin gal tori
Ik faqeer he qurb rasool yara
Jikar hath uthaye dua mange
Kare turt khudaye qabool yara
Vich ghar falaani de rahe khufiya
Khaye ghah te barg babool yara
Sun ke gal mirza gaya ghar andar
Vekhe zaaiqe oh maqbool yara
Fazal shah faqeer ne vekh keha
Vich ghaar aaeyon kit sool yara
