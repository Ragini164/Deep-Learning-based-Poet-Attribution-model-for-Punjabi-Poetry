Charkha sohni da chandan cheer gharreya
Ohdi jurrat sone chandi naal aahi
Maahal phatt di phumnadar sohni
Hor lath kareer di saal aahi
Jiven soorje tarf na dheyaan hove
Aiven husn di zor misaal aahi
Seena saaf si dand balor kolon
Hor sift na vich kheyaal aahi
Sohni naam aaha sohne nain isde
Sohni hans te mor di chaal aahi
Sohne dand rukhsaar anaar wangon
Sohne mukhrre te sohni khaal aahi
Sohne waal kamal daraz usde
Sohne honth surkhi laal o laal aahi
Fazal sach di sohni sohni si
Larri motiyan di waal waal aahi
