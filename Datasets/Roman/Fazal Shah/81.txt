Bas bas miyan maheen chaar nahi
Aiven kha ke namak haram kito
Jis namak khawaleyo ghare us de
Sannh marne da gujha kaam kito
Osse rukh nu wadhna lorreyo ee
Jis rukh de heth aram kito
Dhiyan baitiyan de naal karen haasi
Aese waste tudh ghulam kito
Jis ne paal khavaya gharin is de
Sannh marne da gujha kaam kito
Kha kha ke dudh malaiyan paatt gaeyon
O nakam! eh kaam ki khaam kito
Ki eh samjh daleriyan kitiyan ni
Bhaaven assan taain khas aam kito
Tenu rabb de khof thin jaan ditta
Tu nahi jandon kam jo kham kito
Sadiyan izzatan nu hath paaun laggon
Kha loon haram tmam kito
Bhali niyyat de naal tur jaa ethon
Menu jag de vich badnam kito
Fazal shah aipar ethe rahin nahi
Zahir apna cha anjam kito
