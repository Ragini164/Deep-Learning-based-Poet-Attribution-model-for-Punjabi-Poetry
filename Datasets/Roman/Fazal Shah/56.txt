Tasleemat karke izat baig mirza
Tuhfe nazar guzarda ja miyan
Likh mohar ditti paatshah is nu
Bhara hor ditta saropa miyan
Ghorra sokh samundar ata kita
Sukka sanam langhe dareya miyan
Kita cha muaaf wasool is nu
Kiti hor jageer ata miyan
Pehle hamd khuda baja liyaave
Pher shah di kare sana miyan
Dunya vich kare rab shad tenu
Akhir de jaza khuda miyan
Jamshed fareedon kaaos khusru
Tere martabe di khak paa miyan
Nosherwan wang he adal tera
Hathi kerreyon kare haya miyan
Hatim kul jahan te bakhsh kiti
Aipa aes darbar gada miyan
Ditta rab sikandari mulkh tenu
Wali warsi khalq bana miyan
Sher garg rakhan khof bakri thin
Ditti zul di beekh utha miyan
Mirza paye inaam salam karke
Dere aa varreya naal cha miyan
Nit sair karan dilli sheher andar
Jaa zain samund te paa miyan
Orrak ho udas tyar hoke
Koi roz is tor langha miyan
Kare tarf lahore di kooch mirza
Sara maal mataan lada miyan
Fazal shah lahore de vich mirza
Puhncha manzilo mazali aa miyan
