Kis nu aan ke dhondsen vich nainh de
Wanjhen hath pani jal thal hosi
Kehrra de dilasrra tudh taain
Kis naal saiyaan teri gal hosi
Jadon sohni da koi naam lesi
Tere kaalje de vich sall hosi
Molaa janda he sunji jaan meri
Tera naam lendi kehrre vall hosi
Jehrra botrra aas umeed wala
Soi piyareya oi bajh phal hosi
Tere gaye ni paan kamla saare
Kal vekh lain sukki vall hosi
Jadon sune ga haal asadrre nu
Koila jeev tera jal bal hosi
Fazal shah je aah faghan karsen
Tadon arsh azeem nu hal hosi
