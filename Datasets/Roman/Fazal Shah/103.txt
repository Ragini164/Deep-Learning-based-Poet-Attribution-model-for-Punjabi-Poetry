Murr murr sohni pai pukardi si
Mil jaa oye sajna velrra ee
Okhe waqt aa deh daar menu
Teri chah oye sajna velrra ee
Sunjen rooh ne kul janjaal sutte
Galon laah oye sajna velrra ee
Leher qeher de naal qahar wallon
Peya waah oye sajna velrra ee
Teri sohni ho nidhaal pai aa
Lamme raah oye sajna velrra ee
Sun ke mulk ul maut hairan hoya
Meri aah oye sajna velrra ee
Menu qasam teri leya rok pani
Mera saah oye sajna velrra ee
Mardi waar vekha numaneya nu
Mukh maah oye sajna velrra ee
Jehrra kandhrre te boota jaan da si
Lagi dhah oye sajna velrra ee
Murgh rooh da meer shikar khooni
Leya phaah oye sanja velrra ee
Menu wasl deedar de bajh koi
Nahi chah oye sajna velrra ee
Tere fazal de bajh na hor mera
Dard khaah oye sajna velrra ee
