Ik sheher gujrat jhanaon kandhe
Ohde vich tulla ghumeyar aaha
utam jaat najeeb naseeb wala
Mash'hoor darbar sarkar aaha
Aflatoon luqman shagird usde
Aqal hosh da boht husheyar aaha
Gaviyya cheen da si cheeni saaz tulla
Naal kasab kamal vihaar aaha
Kooze badiya kare tiyar aese
Jis dekheya so talabgar aaha
Jaake nazar guzarda pesh shahaan
Aes chaal kolon maldar aaha
Sara mulk Punjab ohde bhandeyan da
Barre shoq seeti khareedar aaha
Fazal shah har sift masoof tulla 
Naik naam be-ant shumar aaha
