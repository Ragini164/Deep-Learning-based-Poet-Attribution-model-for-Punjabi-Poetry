Vanjhen haath jhanaon andher charrhi
Os raat si aid uchaal hoi
Kacha gharra gudaz hogaya sara
Wahal bhaj gai mande haal hoi
Ghumangher chufer andher kita
Naal leher qaza pamaal hoi
Hun aae ke dekh piyareya oye
Jo kujh main nimaani de naal hoi
Rab kare naseeb na doteyan de
Jo kujh naal mere maheenval hoi
Lakh mauj sandi pai fauj menu
Methon zara na surt sambhaal hoi
Geya paatt jahaz umeed wala
Meri zindagi khuwab kheyal hoi
Sir sir laa bazi tatti khed bethi
Orrak tin kaane meri chaal hoi
Jehrri sohni lai faqeer hoiyon
Churi ajal di naal halal hoi
Chutta teer taqdeer da gheb wallon
Meri jaan oye piyareya dhaal hoi
Kai khe aafat jhanaon sandi
Mere khaan karan khushhaal hoi
Haye! haye! haal mera tenu kon dasse
Fareyad meri vaal vaal hoi
Shala mare is gharra vatendarri da
Jehrri asaan vichorr nihaal hoi
Meri rooh de baaz parvaz kita 
Khali aashiqan hath davaal hoi
Ik waar deedar hun aa deven
Tere mukh di bukh kamaal hoi
Khooni maut da aan ghaneem latha
Sheher aashiqan de harrtaal hoi
Murr ke roz qiyamat de mailrra ee
Eho bhaa asadrre naal hoi
Raj kitiyan na tere naal gallan
Sari umar dukh andar jaal hoi
Hoya sukh naseeb na tatrri nu
Hathon taaneyan jaan vabaal hoi
Dil da khoon akhin bhar ke roni aan main
Mere hanjuwan thin nadi laal hoi
Chori milan aya agge doteyan ton
Hun beliyan gal muhaal hoi
Fazal yaar piyare nu kookadi si
Sohni ghoteyan naal nidhaal hoi
