Sohni roh thin neer palatt aakhe
Bas bas maai matin das nahi
Kahnu sharbay karen naseehtan de
Kujh mujh taain maai das nahi
Kaaon wang maai khado maghaz mera
Tere mukh taain kade bas nahi
Aeven boliyan maar jalavni hain
Bhaven main sohni tere bas nahi
Jichar teek maheenval nu na dekha
Meri rondeyan sikdi ass nahi
Maheenval ton murran muhaal hoya
Gallan das mera jeev khus nahi
Is yaar di qasam na jooth mole
Sunja jeev mera mere wass nahi
Tere zevar zeher misaal menu
Laal sap dass gal hass nahi
Jidhar dekhni aan maheenval disse
Assan kamleyan nu maai hass nahi
Teer ghazab te qeher qaloor wale
Saade maarne nu lak kass nahi
Jaan bujh je karen khuwar menu
Hasil kujh tenu bajh bhas nahi
Dhiyaan bettiyan de parde fash karne
Puttan waleyan de aese jass nahi
Jikar fazal nu cha gawaiyo ee
Badal qeher da wang hun was nahi
