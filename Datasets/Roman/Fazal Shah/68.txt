Mantar lakh karror naseehtan de
Rahi maan ghareeb chala miyan
Kol beth suniyan sabh gallan
Kiti mool na choon charaan miyan
Aipar ishq racheya loon loon sohni de
Jata yaar nu ik khuda miyan
Jis ton jaan imaan qurban kita
Keha is thin pher phira miyan
Ishq nang namoos nu jannda ki
Ishq torrda sharam haya miyan
Fazal shah jo ishq khuwaar hoye
Hathon hovanda doon sawa miyan
