Jadon baap ne gal manzoor kiti
Dasse khol ke kul beyan miyan
Tasleemat karke hath jorr kharra
Pher kare dua pachan miyan
Tooba wang tere saye heth paaye
Kul khalaq jahan imaan miyan
Dil chahnda ae dilli dekhne nu
De hukum baba mehrban miyan
Duja jeev te shoq sodagari da
Jit kit eho nit dheyan miyan
Karaan ja khareed farokht dilli
Naal dostan aqal da taan miyan
Baap sundeyan nal behosh hoya
Goya nikal gai ohdi jaan miyan
Jadon hosh aai zar o zar runna
Izzat baig horaha hairan miyan
Orrak qol qarar na hareyo su
Kare torne da saman miyan
Turat bhej ghulam sada laye
Othaan sane sarbaan miyan
Ditta hukum teyar shataab hovo
Saarban palaan cha paan miyan
Long lachiyan piste saaugi de
Bhare boriyan chak ladaan miyan
Ambar mushk kafoor badaam zafal
Mushk tibti ooth uthaan miyan
Hor sev kharott khajoor ladi
Meve qabuli te khurasaan miyan
Kamkhaab sanjaab samoor makhmal
Latha kamar kh te hor kataan miyan
Sabz surkh siyah hareer ladi
Zari baadla tol halwan miyan
Hor maal na kujh ayaan mere
Ki karan beyaan ayaan miyan
Sara maal asbab ravaan karke
Beta baap doven gharin jaan miyan
Jo kujh baat aahi alfon yey tikar
Is di maai nu beth sunaan miyan
Khushi aish insan de bagh vichon
Ghali aaiyke baad khazan miyan
Maai naal kalejrre naal runni
Tera rab raheem rehmaan miyan
Tudh bajh kis nu sukh chain hosi
Sunja dekh saan kul jahan miyan
Bacha kand na daai nimaneyan nu
Tere bajh jahaan vairan miyan
Karaan cha qurban pardes taain
Meri jaan tu nahi maan taan miyan
Bacha safar nu sikar misaal kehnde
Dilli jaauna nahi asaan miyan
Goya khol kitaab naseehtan di
Rahi maan is nu lakh raan miyan
Akhir ho lachaar kita
Jah rab tera nigehbaan miyan
Ro ro baap te hor ameer sare
Bahir jaake vida karaan miyan
Maan baap nu dard firaq wala
Laga vich kalejrre baan miyan
Kehnda aab te ishq ne khich kiti
Lamme raah ho ture ravaan miyan
Fazal aashiqan murran muhaal hoya
Jinhaan ishq kita preshan miyan
