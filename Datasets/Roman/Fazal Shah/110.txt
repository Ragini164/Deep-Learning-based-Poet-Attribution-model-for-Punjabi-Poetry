Le ja sunehrra sajna da
Aakhin lad gai teri yaar miyan
Hath jorr salam dua kehni
Jana gal na mannon wasaar miyan
Dhahin maar ke arz guzar deni
Agge yaar de banh ulaar miyan
Teri sohni vich jhanaon dubbi
Peya maut da lakh aswar miyan
Assin apni umar nu bhog challe
Tusi jiyo ji saal hazar miyan
Kita boht char yaar milan karan
Tussin suni nakook pukar miyan
Tere ishq nu dagh na laggan ditta
Kiti tusaan te jaan nisar miyan
Fazal shah sohni sohni ho dubbi
Moi qol qarar na haar miyan
