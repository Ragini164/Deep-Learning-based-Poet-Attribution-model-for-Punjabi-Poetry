Suno balkh bukhaare di gal methon
Doven sheher san bohat aabad piyare
Aman chain te raaj chugteyan da
Koi kare na mool fareyad piyare
Ik ghani aaha sakhi mard bhari
Kare nit khudaye nu yad piyare
Vich balakh bukhaare de mehel us de
Aaha maal de naal azad piyare
Nit kare sodagari hor sode
Rakhe peer faqeer nu shad piyare
Hesi zaat da mughal aseel mirza
Aali naam te naik nahad piyare
Jitne lok sodagari karan wale
Sabhnan daye pese sandi daad piyare
Fazal shah sabh cheez mojood aahi
Aipar nahi si ik aulaad piyare
