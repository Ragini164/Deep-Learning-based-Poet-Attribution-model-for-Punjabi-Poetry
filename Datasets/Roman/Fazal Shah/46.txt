Chothe warrhe sone sande karre gharre
Jurre heereyan pinniyan naal miyan
Bazu band badhe upar bazuaan de
utte jurrat jurraaike laal miyan
Ik hass jarrao si choniyan da
Is de gal andar ditta daal miyan
Allah waalrre de kannin waalrre paa
Ditti agg utte agg baal miyan
Zari baadle da gal paa kurta
Utta dain usde laal shaal miyan
Fazal shah na vich kheyal tere
Aesa chamkeya husn jamal miyan
