Warrhe chodhven nu charrhe ghorriyan te
Naal naaz de qadam uthaunda je
Cheeni bhor kameet iraqiyan nu
Hasn mor di chaal sikhaaunda si
Kadi de jolaan medaan jaake
Kadi chaal ravaal kadhaunda si
Karan sair shikaar teyar hoke
Nit baar de val sadhaaunda si
Jehrre mirg nu marg khuda wallon
Soi teer javaan da khaaunda si
Ghorre pherne di laggi waao is nu
Dilli dekhne di chit chaahunda si
Rakh jeev te shoq saudagari da
Yaaran naal salah pakaaunda si
Nit baap de jaa huzoor mirza
Fursat dekh ke sukhan alaaunda si
Aakhe baap ji main ik arz karni
Eho aakh khamosh hojaaunda si
Jekar baap puche dasse mool nahi
Kai roz is tor langhaaunda si
Taahin aakhsan jadon qabool karsen
Aeven baap nu baat sunaaunda si
Fazal shah par ishq ne khich kiti
Mann bhaaunda ji manaunda si

(Es band vich eh tukaan vi mildiyan ne:

Kadi cheene de mukh lagam de ke
Kade zain mushki utte paaunda si
Qismat nal daane pani khich kiti
Likhe lekh na koi mittaunda si)
