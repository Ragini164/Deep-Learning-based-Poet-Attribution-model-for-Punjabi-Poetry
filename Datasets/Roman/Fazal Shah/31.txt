Bazu band aahe band aashiqan de
Lakhan bandeyan nu bandiwaan kita
Kaale kes aahe ohde otar bhinne
Kaale nainan ne dekh tofaan kita
Kai mominan saaf aimaniyan nu
Is di zulf kaali preshan kita
Pazeb fareb de pech paaye
Kann phol malool jahaan kita
Daaoni ghat chaaoni beth husn utte
Rakhi husn da khoob samaan kita
Fazal shah miyan naale patt de ne
Naale patt jahaan veraan kita
