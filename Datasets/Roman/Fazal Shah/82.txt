Maheenval nu jadon jawab milya
Mahin chad ke trunt ravaan hoya
Aakhe rehn keha behn boht mushkil
Mera bhet tmam ayaan hoya
Bajh yaar ujarr chokott disse
Preshan hoya preshan hoya
Okha sajna de gharin jaan hoya
Doti aan khooni asmaan hoya
Jungle jaa raatin kithe baith runna
Aipar rondeyan zor tofaan hoya
Kehrri wal jaavan koi jaa nahi
Tere naam utton qurban hoya
Jidhar jaavna haan udhar milan dhakke
Veri mujh da kul jahaan hoya
Mangi maut na aaundi hath mere
Tera milan okha menu aan hoya
Tudh bajh araam haram menu
Bhala beliya oye peen khaan hoya
Kikar milaan ge das ilaaj koi
Okha aj mera aan jaan hoya
Masjid daaireyan vich bazar phirda
Fazal shah de wang hairan hoya
