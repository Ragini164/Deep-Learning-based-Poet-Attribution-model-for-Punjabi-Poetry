Khat naal maheenval wala parrh ke
Chum naal kalejrre launda je
Likheya yaar aaha mil jaah yara
Yaar yaar karan chit chaunda je
Koi bane na dho milap wala
Orrak ro ke neer vihaunda je
Kehrre tor milsaan piyare yaar taain
Lakh fikar daleel darraunda je
Geya ishq sikha gada hona
Orrak waqt eh ishq sikhaunda je
Pehlon chaak hoya hun khaak mali
Kaaran yaar de bhes vattaunda je
Zalim ishq ne peer faqeer kite
Takhton shah vi chaa giraunda je
Ghar ghar phire bahanrre naal mangda
Kaaran yaar de sagan mauanda je
Koi de gaali koi bura bole
Koi meher seeti kher paunda je
Awwal kul museebtan jhal lenda
Bhar ishq wala jehrra chaaunda je
Ik kher mehboob di chah aahi
Hoi kher bad-kher dharaunda je
Gali jaan wala gali vich kooche
Phire koonj wangon kurlaunda je
Orrak jhaag kooche dukh dard waale
Ghar sehre yaar de jaaunda je
Kher aan faqeer nu daan kijiye
Uchi naal awaz bulaunda je
Kannin yaar di sune awaz sohni
Aipar vich pachan na aunda je
Kaheya sass faqeer nu kher paavin
Sanu aasra fiqr de naam da je
Sohni kher faqeer nu paun challi
Dekho vichrre rab milaunda je
Ohle ho kaheya eh le kher saain
Maheenval romal nu dahunda je
Kher lendeyan saar nisaar hoya
Sara hosh hawas bhulaunda je
Sohni khas misal misal aahi
Aashiq wang patang jalaunda je
Kiti dhoond sohni maheenval bhaven
Jehrra dekh menu ghash khaunda je
Orrak leya pachan te jhass taliyan
Yaar yaar nu tarunt uthaunda je
Gal lag mili bujhi agg dil di
Aipar khof raqeeb di maaon da je
Laggi haal dassan cheti naal sohni
Maheenval vi ro sunaunda je
Kise nanh suni ik doosre di
Orrak kahe sohni mann bhaaunda je
Jaah sidq yaqeen thin beth saaiyba
Aipar paas jo ghatt jhanaon da je
Desi rab murad puga teri
Jehrra kul di aas pajaunda je
Fazal shah mehboob di chah andar
Saat saal misaal langhaunda je
