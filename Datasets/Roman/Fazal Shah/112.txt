Odhar peya udeekda sohni nu
Yaar yaar sanda talab gaar miyan
Nain takde mool na thakde ni
Nargis wang dowen intazar miyan
Ubhe saah le ke maare dhaar munh thin
Naal nain rovan zaar o zaar miyan
Rakhan kul te milan moqoof mera
Aj kiven langhan paar yaar miyan
Pher aakh dende aj kher nahi
Mere ji na chain qarar miyan
Meri jaan faryad faryad kardi
Bhaven yaar nu bani lachaar miyan
Geya bujh chiragh mehboob wala
Bajh yaar hoya dhundkar miyan
Uchanchet pai kann ashiqan de
Ronde rooh di zaar pukar miyan
Maheenval kheyal de naal kaheya
Shayad vichrre ne ehde yaar miyan
Aese haal andar maheenval aaha
Rooh puhncheya ja darbar miyan
Avval de salam piyareyan da
Pichon ro kita izhar miyan
Jo kujh warteya si sir sohni de
Ditta yaar d gosh guzar miyan
Khooni maut orrak piyari sohni nu
Leya vich jhanaon de maar miyan
Sohni dub moi vichkar nain de
Hoi mool na paar uraar miyan
Digga ho behosh khamosh hoke
Maheenval eh sundeyaan saar miyan
Betha puchda haal ehvaal sara
Jadon pher aai is nu saar miyan
Sach akheyo ee ake jhoot kaheyo
Tera nahi painda aitbar miyan
Rooh nabi kareem di qasam khahdi
Yaar jhooth nahi eho kaar miyan
Haye oh dushmanan eh na aakhna si
Khich maardun tez kattar miyan
Sun ke baat mehboob de ghaar wali
Ro ro aakhda hath pasaar miyan
Menu zalima eh ki aakheyo ee
Peya kookda baanh ulaar miyan
Mukh zard hoya dil dard hoya
Lagi kard kalejrre kaar miyan
Jharri la rahe doven nain is de
Saavan meenh jiven abar bahar miyan
Hoya doosri waar behosh murr ke
Kolon rooh hogaya udar miyan
Lagga teer piyareyan sajna da
Awazar hogaya dasaar miyan
Jadon surt aai trunt maar dhahin
Rabb yaad kare waar waar miyan
Ais baat taain karin jhoot rabba
Tera naam he rabb ghaffar miyan
Jikar mareya ee taan bhi mail saaiyan
Ik waar mera dildar miyan
Aiven vavela peya kookda si
De jaah oye yaar deedar miyan
Tere dard firaq ne yaar jani
Laai vich seene kani saar miyan
Disse hath pasariyan mool nahi
Uthe char chufer ghubar miyan
Kuttha yaar oh piyar tusadrre ne
Gal rakh alwar di dhar miyan
Ik waar deedar de kojheyan nu
Mere sohneyan de sardar miyan
Laai saang o peyareya yaar menu
Bannh pattiyan aan sawar miyan
Menu ik ikalleyan chorr ke te
Treyon bannh ke safar de bhaar miyan
Fazal shah nu haal maloom mera
Methon puch na haal nitaar mera
